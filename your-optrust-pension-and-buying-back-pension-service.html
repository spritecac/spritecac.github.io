Did you know that as a member of OPTrust, you may be able to buy back pension service to make up for times when you didn’t contribute to the Plan?

It’s a great way to increase the pension you’ll receive when you retire, because the service you purchase factors directly into the formula used to calculate your pension. And having the added service could help you qualify for an early retirement option.
