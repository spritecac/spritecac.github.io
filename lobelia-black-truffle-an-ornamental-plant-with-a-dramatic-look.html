The uniquely mesmerizing appeal of this ornamental plant comes from its dark chocolaty purple foliage, which helps in adding an exotic look to your surroundings. You can pot the Lobelia “Black Truffle” plant or place it directly in the garden to get a perfectly decorative and enchanting looking garden.
