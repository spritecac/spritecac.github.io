The uniquely mesmerizing appeal of this ornamental plant comes from its dark chocolaty purple foliage, which helps in adding an exotic look to your surroundings. This clump forming herbaceous plant produces bold red flowers which provide a stunning contract to its dark foliage and create a vivid environment around them. You can pot the Lobelia “Black Truffle” plant or place it directly in the garden to get a perfectly decorative and enchanting looking garden.
