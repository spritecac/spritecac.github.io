This video explains the role and benefits of having a school business manager in creating and supporting a world class education system, and gives an introduction to the Institute of School Business Leadership professional Standards. Stephen Morales, Chief Executive of the Institute of School Business Leadership, talks about how effective school business managers and finance directors can help teachers to focus on outcomes for children. More information about the role of the school business manager can be found on Institute of School Business Leadership website https://isbl.org.uk/

This video is part of a suite of information, tools, training and guidance published by the Department for Education to help schools improve their financial management and efficiency. You can find out more at https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/schools-financial-health-and-efficiency.
