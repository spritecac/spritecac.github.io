After a live rendition during their headlining Tomorrowland set featuring an appearance from Kiiara – superstar DJs Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike have released the video for their set in Ibiza, the video depicts the island’s idyllic landscapes with enthralling, contemporary dancers. Kiiara’s bright presence bounces through Ibiza Town as Dimitri Vegas, Like Mike & David Guetta’s Midas touch for beat making forms the perfect musical marriage of 2017 on the pop-meets-dance horizon.
