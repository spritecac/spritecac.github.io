Learn English: May I? For getting permission to do something in our home and outside our home. More Practice: https://youtu.be/UphadASH_1I You will also learn to reply to someone asking you for permission to do something. The reply to, May I? Yes, you may. Each sentence is color coded and illustrated for better understanding. This is an interactive listening and speaking video. This video has no background music. Have fun learning how to use May I and speak English now.

Language in use:

May I turn on the light? Yes, you may.
May I open the door? Yes, you may.
May I have some paper? Yes, you may.
May I have another cup? Yes, you may.
May I borrow your pencil? Yes, you may.
May I play in the sandbox? Yes, you may.
May I turn-off the light? Yes, you may.
