23% of all UK household waste is sent to one of more than 500 landfill sites scattered up and down the country. The waste is then compacted and buried. But these filthy mega mounds of trash aren’t as depressing as they first appear…

Taking a close look at the mega machinery required to handle this much rubbish this video also explores the green recycling efforts used to convert organic food and plant waste into compost which is then sold back to farmers.
