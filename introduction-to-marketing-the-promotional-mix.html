Businesses use promotion to educate, inform, and persuade consumers about themselves, their products, and their services. With a variety of promotional methods available to them, businesses need to develop a strategy so that they promote their products in a unified and effective fashion. This strategy is what’s known as the promotional mix. In this video I’ll describe each element of the promotional mix as well as explain how each is used to enhance a businesses marketing efforts.
