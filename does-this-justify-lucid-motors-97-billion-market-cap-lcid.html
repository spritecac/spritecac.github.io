There is a true and magnificent bull market in EV stock, and it is now underway. The increasing tendency in the remainder of the market seems to be somewhat less significant. In order to get exposure to the industry, investors have poured money into electric vehicle start-ups. Companies like Lucid, which went public via a special purpose acquisition company, have benefited from this trend.
