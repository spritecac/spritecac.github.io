With this song I really tried to put myself in the mind frame of someone who just got out of a relationship, but isn’t necessarily upset with their former partner, and is instead more upset with with their self for letting themself grow away from the person they once loved so dearly.

With this video, I attempted to demonstrate the isolation that comes with coming out of a relationship, and the attempt that one makes to try and entertain themselves, and give themself new purpose.
