"Hello, dear friends! I have a new portion of English for you today! Today it’s an expression: "To take into account". To take into account means to remember to consider someone or something when making a plan for the future. Imagine a conversation between two business partners:
- I think we are finally ready with our plan for Ukraine.
- Hey, but have you taken into account the upcoming elections there?
- Oh, yeah, that is a good idea to think about.
