The Mach-E GT is Ford’s performance EV to battle the Tesla Model Y & Audi e-Tron. For 2022 Shelby American wants to take it to the next level. Powering the Shelby Mach-E GT are (2) electric motors that produce 480HP & mated to a direct drive transmission. Is the 2022 Shelby Mach-E GT an electric performance Mustang you would BUY?
