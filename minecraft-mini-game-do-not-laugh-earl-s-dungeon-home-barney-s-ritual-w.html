Hey recruits, welcome back to another Minecraft Do Not Laugh video! Today SkyDoesMinecraft, ThatGuyBarney, House_Owner, and RedVacktor play their Minecraft mini-game Do Not Laugh! Barney starts out in the center and Sky begins the game strong with a surprise filming of Bacon Man and Pancake Pal. Ross is confident that he can get Sky to laugh, and he wins title of Longest Possible Preparation. Sky gets tired of waiting and spoils the joke for himself, and our innocent Office Wizard Jake pays the price. Sky gets revenge with some Fisk Surprise Eggs. Moving on, Red gets Ross with a good old fashioned Minecraft NASCAR joke. While Barney preps for his joke, Sky and Ross argue about points and Red plants a field. Finally Barney leads Red into his chamber, where he’s performing a terrifying ancient Minecraftian ritual to summon Goatman. When Ross appears sporting a fashionably scary goat skin, Barney tells him of the woes of his own goat – it talks back to him and he must hit it every day. Goatman isn’t happy to hear of this mistreatment of a goat, and Barney decides to end his own life. Sky performs the ritual to return the Goatman to his homeworld, but he messes it up with Fisk Eggs. Sky advises Barney not to touch his ice cream, and when Barney doesn’t listen, Sky morphs into an ice cream cone. With Ross’ help, Sky, Barney, and Red stumble onto Earl the Dungeon Beast’s house, where Earl requests that they clean out his torture dungeon so he can harvest fresh ingredients. Barney sets up a long winded pitch that ends in him trying to sell Ross a ceiling fan. Red gets Barney (and everyone else) as a mysterious, ugly mole-like creature that can only walk on its hind legs while saying “wiggle wiggle wiggle”. Sky tries to kill the beast with his bow, but to no avail. Ross saves the day the only way he knows how – he spawns a fighter jet, but Red eats it. Ross takes Barney on a walk to an island of TNT, but everyone is left confused after it blows up. Ross reveals that he couldn’t think of a joke, so he thought an explosion could be kind of funny. He was wrong. Ross, to everyone’s despair, brings The Game back. That’s it for this episode of Minecraft Do Not Laugh – thanks for watching!
