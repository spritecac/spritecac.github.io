Dr. Makrypoulias is the head of the anesthesiologist team of the VIP-Rhinoplasty Center. He is responsible to deliver a completely secure and painless anesthesia for a rhinoplasty operation.
Dr. Makrypoulias is fully informed about every patient’s medical history just after their consultation with Dr. Mireas.
Normally, pre-op examination is performed 2-3 hours before the surgery. In cases of severe cardia, lung or other problems an appointment with the anesthesiologist is booked by us some days prior to the surgery date.
Just before the operation, Dr. Makrypoulias and Dr. Mireas pass by patient’s room to give some last-minute instructions. Then in the OR you can see how our Dr.Makrypoulias makes sure that the operation is going to be performed under the best possible conditions.
