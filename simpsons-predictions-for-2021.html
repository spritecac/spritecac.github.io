The Simpsons have long been prolific projectors into the future of world events from the Super Bowl, to grease stealing, to Trump becoming President. I’m not talking about the time Lisa projected the Simpson world growing a fifth finger either. Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, Maggie and the whole of Springfield has turned out to be right for many moments in history.
