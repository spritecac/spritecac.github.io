This is the revised Skoda Superb Estate!

It’s the sort of car you’d be considering if you were in the market for an affordable yet spacious estate, so something like a Volkswagen Passat Estate or Peugeot 508 SW. The question is, does the revised Superb offer enough to make it a suitable choice in comparison to the competition?

Well, one key area where the Superb succeeds is the boot, with the 660 litres of storage making it the class-leader in this category! But that’s not the only impressive element… There’s a wide choice of trims and engines, including 3 petrols, 3 diesels and even a plug-in hybrid!

So it all sounds pretty impressive, and with a starting price of under £27,000 it’s more than affordable! So is it the all-round class-leaders for affordable family estate cars? You’ll have to keep watching to find out!
