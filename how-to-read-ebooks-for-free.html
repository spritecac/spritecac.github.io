My go-to way to escape the coronavirus pandemic has been to crack open a good book and get lost in its pages. In the video above, I share how you can get your hands on eBooks without spending a dime.

Use your library card

Arthur was right: Having fun isn’t hard when you’ve got a library card. Apps like Libby (iOS, Android), OverDrive (iOS, Android), and Hoopla Digital (iOS, Android) make it incredibly easy to borrow digital books using your library card. Libby can even connect to your Amazon account to automatically send books you’ve borrowed to your Kindle device.
