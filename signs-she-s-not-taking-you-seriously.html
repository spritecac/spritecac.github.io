Signs she’s not taking you seriously
Hello guys, it’s me Gabriella your favorite girl from Kamalifestyle. In today’s video, I’ll take you through a rather sensitive topic on signs that she is not taking you seriously.
Now, it is important to feel appreciated and wanted by the woman you love as much as you appreciate them in every relationship. We want to be held as seriously as we take out partners, and that is why I’m here with some tell-tale signs on whether she is taking you seriously or not. But guys before I continue why don’t you hit that lovely subscribe button and turn on the notifications so you don’t miss out on the next coming video.
1. She never texts or call first.
This has to be the easiest way to know if the girl you are dating or crushing on is not taking you seriously. Keeping in touch is one way through which human beings form emotional connections with each other. Therefore, if she only talks to you when you do, it is a clear sign that she is not as interested in you. Think of it this way, your crush or girlfriend should be excited and eager to talk to you whenever possible. If she is not, there is a high chance she is not in it for the long run.
2. Delays in texting or calling you back
Secondly, you can know that she is not taking you seriously when she overly delays returning your calls or texts. Keep in mind that some girls can be genuinely busy, and hence their delay is justified. However, no one is always ever busy for 24 hours.
What’s more, like men, women also make time in their busy schedules for things and people that matter to them. So, therefore, if she can’t spare a few minutes to call or text back, it is because she does not want to.
