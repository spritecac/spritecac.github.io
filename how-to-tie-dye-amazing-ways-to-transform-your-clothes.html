Hey crafters :) In this video, you will see many ideas you can use to renovate or transform your boring clothes into fantastic fashion pieces. A super easy way to re-do your clothes and add more bright colors is to put some rubber bands on them and soak them in dye. You can use as many rubber bands as you want and put them on your clothes differently to create unusual and unique ornaments. Mix bleach with cornstarch and put them inside a piping bag.
