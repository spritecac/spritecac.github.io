“Extra” Special Correspondent Samantha Harris spoke with Kit Harington at the Los Angeles premiere of “Eternals,” where he discussed reuniting with his “Game of Thrones” co-star Richard Madden on the movie.

Despite sharing very little screen time together, Kit said, “Working on this together was pretty special… If you added our friendship time, it’s a lot more.”

“Eternals” hits theaters November 5.
