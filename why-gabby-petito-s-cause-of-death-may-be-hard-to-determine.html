Determining how Gabby Petito died will likely be a challenge given the amount of time that has passed. Though the FBI announced the discovery of human remains that resembled Petito’s description in Grand Teton National Park, the 22-year-old has not been seen since August 25. The remains were found in a remote camping area and were likely exposed to the elements, including intense heat and animals.
