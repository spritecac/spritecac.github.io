In this episode of The Chop Up, Dan and Dustin welcome Made By James to the show.

James Martin (aka Made By James) is a logo designer based out of the UK and one of the more popular logo designers on Instagram. We’ve been fans of him for quite a while so it was an honor to be contacted by him to kick in some designs for his brand.

We also talk about his new book which is coming out in November! Pre order his book from his website:

https://www.madebyjames.co/made-by-james-book
