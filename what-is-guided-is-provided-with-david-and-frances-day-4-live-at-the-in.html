In the morning of day four of the Into the Kingdom retreat, Svava starts off singing a song she wrote called, "Beautiful Day." Afterward, David and Frances come in and David starts off sharing about the theme of compromise in the split mind. He delivers a strong context around the illusion of the body and how there is only one true use for the body, and that is the Holy Spirit’s purpose of communication.

Towards the end of the session, David brings in some other members of the Living Miracles Community to share a bit of their journey and some of the insights they’ve had along the way.

The session wraps up with Neda Boin singing a song she wrote called, “I Am Not a Body.”

Other themes/topics included in this talk: The meaning of the crucifixion, the connection between guilt and specialness, what is consciousness, how the Living Miracles community works and how it came about, the origins of the La Casa de Milagros center in Mexico, and how what is guided is provided.

This session was recorded during the Into the Kingdom retreat in the morning of October 10, 2019, at the Castle De Berckt in Holland.
