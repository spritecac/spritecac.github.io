As a dedicated DJ/producer Veleyle is from Istanbul and embraced the richness of sounds in this region. She blends ethnic instruments and rich percussions with house groove and demystifies sonic exploration of psychedelic synths and authentic vocals with the contemporary form of electronic music. She is inspired by a la turca music, ancient chants, oriental tunes and folk melodies from all over the world.
