From there a long stretch all across the Hudson Bay took us to Meadowbank Gold Mine where our relatively heavy jet landed on a GRAVEL STRIP! The return flights, which are also released on Air-Clips.com, took us all the way back with a fuel stop in Renkin Inlet and then back to Mirabel via Val-d’Or.
