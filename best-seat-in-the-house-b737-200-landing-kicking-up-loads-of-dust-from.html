The AirClips.com Team had the fantastic opportunity to join Nolinor Chief Pilot Captain Jean-Christophe "JC" and his First Officer Pierre-Charles on a very very special mission on their classic 1983-built ex-Royal Air Maroc Boeing 737-200 Combi aircraft with an upgraded "glass cockpit". The trip started at the company’s home base in Montreal Mirabel with only passengers, but not yet cargo on board. After less than an hour we landed in Val-d’Or for adding two pallets of cargo. From there a long stretch all across the Hudson Bay took us to Meadowbank Gold Mine where our relatively heavy jet landed on a GRAVEL STRIP! The first two legs will be shown in each one ULTIMATE COCKPIT MOVIE. The return flights, which are also released on Air-Clips.com, took us all the way back with a fuel stop in Renkin Inlet and then back to Mirabel via Val-d’Or. This is an exclusive scene from the second full film about the second leg from Val-d’Or to Meadowbank! Enjoy AMAZING sights along the trip!
