Drawing from his daily practice since 2002, he helps people break through common obstacles and devises ways to address attention-related challenges everyone faces. Daron compares mindfulness practice to physical fitness to help distinguish the benefits from the exercises required to experience them. Daron Larson helps people feel more at home in their lives by encouraging them to habitually notice ordinary experiences in liberating ways.
