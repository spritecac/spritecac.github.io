2022 is less than 3 months away, so that is why I decided to take a closer look into the up and coming trends for spring and summer 2022. It is easy to notice that the Y2K fashion is going to be the IT-trend next year. And then we have some trends that have been sticking around for some seasons, for example cut-outs, fringe, sheer fabrics and crochet. I hope you will enjoy this video and let me know what you think!
