There are still thousands of restored cars left all over the world. Cars that are preserved because the Charger has become so populair. Cars that otherwise would have rotten away in some junkyard in the last 50 years. So I think it’s safe to say that Dukes of Hazzard was actually a GOOD thing to happen to Dodge’s Charger.
