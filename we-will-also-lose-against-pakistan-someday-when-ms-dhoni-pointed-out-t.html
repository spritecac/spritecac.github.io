During the 2016 T20 World Cup, MS Dhoni had pointed out that although India have an unbelievable record against Pakistan in World Cups, the streak will end one day.

"We are proud of the fact that we are 11-0 up. But it is a reality that we will also lose at some point of time, if it’s not today, then it will be after 10, 20 or 50 years."
