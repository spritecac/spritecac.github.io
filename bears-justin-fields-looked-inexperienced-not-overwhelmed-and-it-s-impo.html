All Chicago Bears fans are waiting to see who starts at quarterback on Sunday, will it be veteran Andy Dalton or rookie Justin Fields. The fans want Fields but the Bears’ brass has remained steadfast that Dalton is the starter. Fields played most of the Week 2 game against the Bengals and showed flashes, but it was far from a convincing performance to leave no doubt he is the starter. NBC Sports Chicago Bears Insider Adam Hoge notes that while Fields looked inexperienced, he did not look overwhelmed and that is an important difference when assessing Fields’ readiness.
