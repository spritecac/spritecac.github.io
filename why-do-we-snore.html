Snoring can cause sleep disturbance for both the snorers and those around them. If severe it can also be a warning sign for obstructive sleep apnoea, a disease that causes people to intermittently struggle or stop breathing during the night. Receiving help for such sleep related breathing disorders is essential. But don’t worry, snoring and sleep apnoea can be treated!
