The #english #word of the day is rapidly. #vocabulary

Rapidly means quickly or very fast
For example:
1 - If you saw a bird fly by very quickly, you can say, “ Wow! That bird flew rapidly across the lawn and towards the trees.”
2 - She spoke rapidly with excitement when she found out that her favourite movie was releasing today.
3 - The words moved rapidly across the screen and I was not able to read them clearly.
Next time when something is moving very quickly, try using the word rapidly instead.
