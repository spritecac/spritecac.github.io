Dave Rubin of The Rubin Report talks to Candace Owens about why she started growing her own food, why the right should have lockdown regret, why Russell Brand is better than Ana Kasparian, and how the politics of fear are being used against you. Candace talks about why cities like Los Angeles and New York City are dying and why Tennessee has a much brighter future. She discusses how big government has used the fear of COVID to launch a massive assault on our freedoms and civil liberties. She also explains how conservatives made a massive mistake to allow lockdowns at the beginning of the pandemic and why they bear some of the responsibility for the amount of freedom we’ve lost. Candace explains why many people seem eager to let the government tell them how to live their lives and why freedom is hard, but worth it. She also discusses how we went from the greatest generation to the most pathetic generation and how she became so fearless. Candace also hints at what the future may hold for her pursuing public office. Candace also describes why people should not fear their government officials like Ted Lieu and instead they should fear us. Candace also describes the key difference between liberals like Ana Kasparian and Russell Brand. Candace also talks about how the mainstream media has gone from an institution that challenged the government to one that is in the service of it. Finally, she gives her thoughts on what the next attempt to seize our freedoms might look like.
