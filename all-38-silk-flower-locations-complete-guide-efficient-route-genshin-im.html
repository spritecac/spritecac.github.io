► Description:
10 of the Silk Flowers must be purchased for a price of 1000 Mora per Silk Flower in two different shops. The respawn and restock time for Verr Goldet’s shop are both 48 hours. The restock time for Ms. Bai’s shop is 72 hours. Silk Flowers are used as character ascension materials for the following characters: Xingqiu, an amazing 4-Star Hydro Support character and Hu Tao (rumored), the upcoming 5-Star Pyro character. It takes 168 Silk Flowers to reach Ascension Level 6 so farm early!
