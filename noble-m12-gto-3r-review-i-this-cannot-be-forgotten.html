Today on Feathers and Tarmac, we review the beast that redefined automotive history in every way possible and gave a new face to what a true drivers car should always be. Today, we review the Noble M12 GTO 3R.

FULL MODDED SPEC:

-500 bhp at 1.1 bar of boost

-custom forged pistons

-Garrett t28 turbos modified by Owen Developments as hybrids

-Tial 44mm external waste gates

-uprated exhaust manifolds

-Exhausts straight from turbo to floor

-Modified track oil sump holds 8.5 litres of oil

-Pro alloy oil separation tank

-Pro alloy coolant expansion tank

-Pro alloy intercooler kit

-Pro alloy Intercooler piping kit

-uprated fuel injectors

-Getrag 6 speed manual gearbox uprated internals (competition)

-gearbox oil cooler (switch on panel)

-Carbon front splitter & rear diffuser tuxman

-Carbon fibre wing end plates

-M400 side scoops

-Carbon fibre front floor extensions

-Carbon fibre Radiator Gurney

-Rear clam modified

-Nitron r3 coilovers

-uprights machined and adjustable

-Noble racing alloys

-Alcon uprated front/rear discs
