The rise of online shopping, a preference for specialty retailers and the decline of shopping malls have contributed to department stores falling out favour with shoppers. Marketing and retail expert Dr Louise Grimmer will examine the hey-day of department stores, and some of the factors that have contributed to the decline of the sector. The presentation will conclude by reflecting on what the future might hold for department stores.
