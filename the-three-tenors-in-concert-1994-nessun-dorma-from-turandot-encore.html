As the encore of their magnificent 1994 concert, The Three Tenors joined together for Puccini’s "Nessun Dorma", made famous in pop culture by Luciano Pavarotti himself. Watch the full performance during its 25th anniversary year: https://w.lnk.to/The3TenorsLY

The Three Tenors – Plácido Domingo, José Carreras, and Luciano Pavarotti – along with conductor Zubin Mehta, the L.A. Philharmonic, and the L.A. Music Center Opera Chorus put on the show of a lifetime at Los Angeles’s Dodger Stadium during this legendary concert, preceding the 1994 World Cup.

A Tibor Rudas production.
