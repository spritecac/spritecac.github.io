The expansive new setting of Mexico really does have so much to offer, and longtime players and newcomers alike are sure to have a blast discovering everything you can get stuck into. I’ve lost count of the amount of races I sped my way through that I immediately wanted to experience all over again. I’m already itching to get right back into the driver’s seat.
