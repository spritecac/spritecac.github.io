Computer system components are all around us in todays world in devices such as smartphones tablets and laptops. All of these devices have properties of a computer system. Computing power is built in to everyday items such as cars and microwaves.
It is worth understanding how computing works through input processing and output. This requires hardware devices to enable human interaction and programs (software) to allow computers to follow instructions.
