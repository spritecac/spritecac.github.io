How To Prepare For An Informal Interviews

Nowadays informal interviews are quite common, but what is informal interview?? instead of structured, formal interview in the conference room it is taken at a coffee shop, local restaurant or at the bookstore.

"let’s meet over a coffee or a lunch" you may get interview call like this, this type of interview normally occurs while hiring a manager or any high skilled candidate. but why like this? it is to see if you are fit the culture of their organisation.

Informal interview saves time and money of recruiting process and also clarify thing between recruiter and candidate.
