Divine is also featured on the OUTLAWZ (Tupac) track "Good-Bye", the song "Good & Plenty" on the DEEP BLUE SEA Soundtrack, and the song Fairy Tales on the LOVE STINKS Soundtrack. Group members include Kia Thornton, Nikki Bratcher, and Tonia Tash - All three women were teenagers when they were signed by Red Ant/Pendulum Records-BMG.
