Flavour profile,
Black liquorice root and milk chocolate is blended with flue cured Virginia tobacco that has a mild ashy flavour with underlying chocolate and aniseed flavour.

Pipe – Presbyterian

Rum & Raisin Tobacco

Blended smooth naturally sweet tobacco has been blended with dark rum and raisins with back notes of chocolate, it’s not too sweet so that you can vape it all day.
