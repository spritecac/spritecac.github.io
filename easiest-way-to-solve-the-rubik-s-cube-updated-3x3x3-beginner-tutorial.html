TIME STAMPS & ALGORITHMS

——————————————————-
Basic Notation and the Pieces 00:43
——————————————————-

Example: R L U D F B
R = Turn the right face Clockwise and R’ = Turn the right face Counter Clockwise

Step 1 - Solving the White Cross - 03:07
——————————————————-
Step 2 - Solving the White Corners - 07:23
——————————————————-

Here are a few scenarios for the placement of your white corner piece:

1) Bottom right with the white facing right - 08:12
2) Bottom right with the white facing us - 09:14
3) Top left with the white facing us - 09:34
4) Bottom left with the white facing down - 10:59
5) Bottom right with the white facing down - 12:21
6) Top right with the white facing us - 13:22
7) All corners completed except one
Bottom right with the white facing down - 14:06
8) All corners have a white piece facing up
Two or more pieces need to be swapped - 14:42

——————————————————-
Step 3 - Solving the Middle Layer - 15:59
——————————————————-

Top to Right - U R U’ R’ F R’ F’ R - 17:22
Top to Left - U’ L’ U L F’ L F L’ - 18:28
If all the edge pieces are yellow - 20:49

——————————————————-
Step 4 - Solving the Yellow - 23:00
——————————————————-

Here are a few scenarios of a yellow pattern you may have:

1) Hook - F U R U’ R’ F’ - 23:26
2) Bar - F R U R’ U’ F’ - 24:06
3) Lonely Center - Do the hook or the bar move - 24:43
4) Overlapping Squares - R U R’ U R U2 R’ - 26:05
5) Sign - R U R’ U R U2 R’ - 26:36
6) Cross - R U R’ U R U2 R’ - 27:07
7) Fish with the yellow facing us - R U R’ U R U2 R’ - 27:48
8) Fish with the yellow facing right - Y’ L’ U’ L U’ L’ U2 L - 29:26 (Left Sune)

——————————————————-
Step 5 Part 1 - Solving the Corners - 30:08
——————————————————-

Here are three scenarios you may have:

1) Three of the same colors in the top row - 30:23
2) Two of the same color corner pieces - 30:41
Orient the corners to their same color and have that side face back and then do this move - R’ F R’ B2 R F’ R’ B2 R2

3) No matching color corner pieces - 31:28
Doesn’t matter the orientation, just do this move R’ F R’ B2 R F’ R’ B2 R2. Then find the side with the completed corners and have that side facing back then do the same move again.

——————————————————-
Step 5 Part 2 - Solving to Completion - 33:05
——————————————————-

Here are four scenarios you may have:

1) Swap the four edges opposite - M2 U M2 U2 M2 U M2 - 33:32
2) Swap the four edges adjacent - M2 U M2 U M’ U2 M2 U2 M’ U2 - 34:10
3) Swap the three edges clockwise - R2 U R U R’ U’ R’ U’ R’ U R’ - 35:15
4) Swap the three edges counter clockwise - R U’ R U R U R U’ R’ U’ R2 - 36:03
