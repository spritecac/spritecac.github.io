After a long wait, join us at 7pm tonight for the world premiere of Searching for Greta.

This original musical, written by Mr. Garbe, Mr. Zero and CDS alumna Julia Garbe ’17, is full of zany characters just waiting to be brought to life for the very first time. It promises to be a hilarious, memorable and moving journey that will challenge cast, crew and audience members alike to consider the positive steps they can take to help tackle the climate crisis.

Congratulations to the cast and crew for an incredible show that we all hope you enjoy.
