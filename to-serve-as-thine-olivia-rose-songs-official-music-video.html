To Serve as Thine!

I remember the day I wrote this song - we had just finished reading in Hebrews and my mind was filled with the wonder of Jesus’ humility.

The Son of God, the Perfect Lamb, crowned with all glory and honor, walked the dirt roads in sandaled feet to SAVE you and I. He didn’t go to the "religious" men, He went to the un-lovely, the hurting, the lost.

He lived it. What an inspiration.
