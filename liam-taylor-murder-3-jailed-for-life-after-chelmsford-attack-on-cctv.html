A community was left mourning the death of 19-year-old boy after another teenager fell victim to a knife attack on Essex’s streets.

The final moments of Liam’s life were caught on camera by the pub.

Emergency services rushed to The Green in Writtle at around 8pm on Friday (January 31) 2020 following reports that two men had been attacked outside the Rose and Crown pub.

Sadly, Liam Taylor was pronounced dead at the scene.

The second victim, also 19, was taken to hospital with non life-threatening injuries. He has since been discharged from hospital.

Ryan Filby and Daniel Daden were convicted of murder and wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm on 5 May following a trial. Louis Colgate admitted Liam’s murder on 15 January, but this can only be reported now following the lifting of reporting restrictions.

“Liam’s murder has caused reverberations throughout the community and among those who knew him, and I understand people will be angry, upset and grieving.
