When it comes to new tunes, we try to stay on top of it all. Bobby Raps & Corbin are two extraordinary artists.

On 6/1/2015, The two paired up to drop "Couch Potato" out of the blue.

The EP features 7 tracks, all producer by Bobby Raps himself.

The two artists really compliment each others styles, with Corbin having a smooth voice with a mix of grunge-rap mixed with some incredbile singing, while Bobby comes in with some insane flows that leave the beat absolutely destroyed. The incredible emotion that these two artists convey when they rap will leave one turning all the way up.

Bobby Raps has a knack at producing tracks, with ambient filled tracks that have some greased up synths to compliment it’s spacey vybe.

Do not sleep on these two artists, members of The stand4rd, a group composed of like-minded artists who are slowly but surely showing up on the scene.
