Coco and Hugh lived in the property for only a couple of year but coco put her mark on this fabulous abandoned mansion which can still be seen to this very day!
This mansion used to house great guest like Sir Winston Churchill who regularly would spend his time there hunting and fishing with coco and Hugh.
Sadly this brilliant architecture has sat empty since 1967 on a 700 acre site exposed to elements early last year the property was sold for ¬£3 million and will be turned into a Hotel in the future..
