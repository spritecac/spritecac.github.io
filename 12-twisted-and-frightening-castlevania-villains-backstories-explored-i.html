If you are into anime, you just cannot possibly miss out on Castlevania! No, we are not talking about the video game here, we are stressing on Netflix’s Castlevania anime series. Spanning across four seasons with 32 very exciting episodes, Warren Ellis’ Castlevania has been a commercial as well as a critical success.
