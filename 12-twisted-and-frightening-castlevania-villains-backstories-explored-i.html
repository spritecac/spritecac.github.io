If you are into anime, you just cannot possibly miss out on Castlevania! No, we are not talking about the video game here, we are stressing on Netflix’s Castlevania anime series. Okay, it is true that this American anime-influenced adult animated dark fantasy action television series initially began as a loose adaptation of Konami’s Japanese video game series also called Castlevania but fans who have streamed it online will know that the series has successfully been able to construct a world of its own backed by a remarkable work of animation and some riveting character dynamics of course. Spanning across four seasons with 32 very exciting episodes, Warren Ellis’ Castlevania has been a commercial as well as a critical success.
Well, one of the primary reasons why the series has been such a hit is solely because of the characters on display and by characters, we are more inclined towards the villains who are featured here. While full credits have to be given to the main antagonist of the series, Dracula, Castlevania does have a lot more to offer.
