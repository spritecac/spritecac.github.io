Building Your Own Dream Home: Q&A with Josh Fitzgerald of Archier, Casa Acton
READ MORE: https://www.huntingforgeorge.com/blog/casa-acton-archier-let-us-in-home-tour/

We recently took you on a Let Us In tour of Casa Acton, a moveable 53sqm bush dwelling in Tasmania. Casa Acton is a truly remarkable build that was completed by the home owner and architect, Josh Fitzgerald of Archier. With a design that centred around simplicity and sustainability, this clever piece of architecture merges small living with luxurious detailing. We spoke candidly with Josh about the build and what challenges he faced along the way. From a 9 month long affair with sandstone to his plans for future extensions. Go behind the scenes with us and hear more of Josh’s design journey…
