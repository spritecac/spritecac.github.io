The planets didn’t quite align for the launch of Elite Dangerous Odyssey, and whilst the game still has a long way to go, Frontier have been making some progress. Looking towards the future, it appears as though Frontier might finally be treating Elite as a live-service game.

Today, we are seven updates after the Odyssey launch. Two further updates have been announced, with Update 8 focusing on mega-ship interiors, and 9 focusing on Fleet Carrier Interiors and a new SRV. It seems very likely then, that this maybe the trend that Frontier continue to follow, with a new update every 4 to 6 weeks. If that is to be the case, then what could Update 10, 11 and beyond bring?

Here’s a few ideas I want to put out there. Keep in mind that these are entirely suggestions, and that Frontier haven’t confirmed or even hinted at anything like this. Bottom line, here are a few things I would like to see that I feel would improve Odyssey dramatically.
