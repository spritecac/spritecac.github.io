Eskisehir - a city that can not be described by words but
Only felt by heart. It’s a place that showed me a side of Turkey that I haven’t seen before. With 3 big universities, there are a lot of students and young people around which makes this a very lively and colorful place with lots of entertainment.

Also turkish legends like Nuri Alço and Cuneyt Arkin are true Eskisehir’lis which is proof that it’s a really cool city :)

While in Eskisehir I noticed military planes flying above the city which really freaked me out, but nobody else seemed to be bothered by it. That’s when I understood that it might be pretty normal in Eskisehir.

The name Eskişehir literally means "Old City" in Turkish and with that being said, there is a lot of history in the province. My journey takes me to a place 65km south and 2500 years back in history…
