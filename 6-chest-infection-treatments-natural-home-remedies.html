What home remedies can you take for a chest infection?

This video shares helpful chest infection treatments such as:

- a hot lemon and honey drink
- get plenty of rest
- drink lots of water
- take painkillers
- elevate your chest with extra pillows
- use your inhaler (if asthmatic)

IMPORTANT: if left untreated, some chest infections can become life-threatening. For more information on when to see a doctor if you have a chest infection, visit: https://www.medicspot.co.uk/treatment/chest-infection
