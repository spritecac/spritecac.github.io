In represent of Ibiza Sonica:
Way of Thinking is the combination of two veteran artists with different experiences and backgrounds that destiny has crossed them in their professional careers. On the one hand Javier Ferrer, CEO and Sound Engineer at Métrica Recording Studio in Ibiza and General Manager of the Ushuaïa and Hï Ibiza studios with more than 20 years in production and on the national scene. On the other hand, Álvaro Mesa, one of the leading figures in the south of Spain and the head and resident of Fiesta&Bullshit platform, is also the Artistic Director of the Sun and Snow Festival in Sierra Nevada. Their combined qualities combine perfectly both behind the plates and in the studio work. Its soul and identity are the enveloping melodies and atmospheres with resounding drums, always betting on what is different, elegant and unique. The decision to join begins at the beginning of this year 2020 with a great number of expectations from the national scene placed on them. Nowadays they already have the support of big names of the industry and their tracks are in labels like Nazca Records or Métrica Music.
