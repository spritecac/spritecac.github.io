I am loving this adventure with Acrylic Paint that I am on right now!. I hope you are too. OH MY GOSH I got cells without silicon or torching! This is similar to the effect you get with the Swipe Method but with all the colors showing. So very exciting!

I’m pouring the paint mixtures on Freezer paper so aft everything is dry I can pull up the acrylic skin to make jewelry too.

This time we are using some gesso primed canvas sheet, Yupo synthetic paper and freezer paper. It is like being a kid again and paying with the colors, seeing what happens. I love that feeling.

There’s no silicon oil or torching in my projects as I really worry about the possible side effects of burning the acrylic paints and the materials they are made from. We all have to weigh the pros and cons of how we interact with our art supplies. Experimenting can be exciting and beautiful but there can be consequences we don’t know about yet. As with any thing in life be informed by reputable sources and your own common sense. :)

In this video I’m playing with leftover paints from several projects my husband and I have been doing lately. All of the paint has PVA (Elmer’s Glue All) GAC-800, acrylic paint or India inks and water.

The most important things you need to be successful with any of my creative projects are the willingness to learn and to be easy on yourself as you are learning. We tend to be our worst critics and we need to learn to be kind to ourselves.
