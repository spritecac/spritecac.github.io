Hey Guys, In this video I take a zero day off the bike and explore Ouray, Colorado. I have been wanting to come to this town for close to 8 years now. Ouray is really something special, tucked in The San Juans at the start of a valley. Ouray is the start of The Million Dollar Highway and Americas Most Dangerous Road, Ouray is a large part of why i’m bicycle touring Thru the San Juans. I spent the day walking the streets, finding all the waterfalls, and hiking on The Perimeter Trail, There is so much to do and see in this section of The Rocky Mountains. I will definitely be back to Ouray to hike those Mountain Peaks. Stay tuned Everybody and see you on the road.
