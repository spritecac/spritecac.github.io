02-OCT-2021. A Spirit Airlines Airbus A320neo (A20N), registration N922NK, performing flight NKS3044 / NK3044 from Atlantic City International Airport, NJ (USA) to Fort Lauderdale International Airport, FL (USA) was accelerating for takeoff from RW 31 when the pilots rejected takeoff and reported a bird strike.
