Then Alex travels into the future to discover how the past can be changed. There, he meets a librarian, to whom he informs that the time machine has not yet been invented. Then he travels to the year 2037, where he meets a community of ancient people who don’t understand his language, but a girl named ‘Mara’ saves him. Later, Alex discovers that there are some creatures who have taken the people from the community for their survival, and they have also taken Mara with them inside the earth.
