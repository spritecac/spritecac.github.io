Then Alex travels into the future to discover how the past can be changed. There, he meets a librarian, to whom he informs that the time machine has not yet been invented. Then he travels to the year 2037, where he meets a community of ancient people who don’t understand his language, but a girl named ‘Mara’ saves him. Later, Alex discovers that there are some creatures who have taken the people from the community for their survival, and they have also taken Mara with them inside the earth. Alex wanted to save Mara, but the people in the community were unwilling to assist him.

Mara’s brother informs him that humankind divided into two groups and there is spirit who is aware of it; he was referring to the librarian, who has survived for many years and is considered a ghost.
