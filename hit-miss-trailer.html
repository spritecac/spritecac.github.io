The letter reveals that Mia has an 11-year-old son from their former relationship and in her will, Wendy has appointed Mia the legal guardian of this child and three other half-siblings.

Still struggling to establish her own identity, Mia drives out to the Yorkshire countryside and meets the confused, resentful children.
