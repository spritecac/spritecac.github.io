This is an #ad for Fisher-Price. Alex, Ellie, and Eric pretend to play with the Thomas & Friends Trains & Cranes Super Tower! They go on exciting races, chases, and even load and unload cargo, just like on the show. Available now at Walmart, Target, and Amazon!

The Trains & Cranes Super Tower is a multi-level Thomas & Friends™ playset standing over 2.5 feet tall and featuring favorite places and characters from the show. Includes Tidmouth Sheds with spinning turntable, Brendam Docks with Cassia, Carly crane, and Cranky the Crane™, and handles to control where engines go. Compatible with other Thomas & Friends™ tracks, except wood. Connect and expand the railway play! (Additional track sold separately and subject to availability.) It also comes with a motorized Thomas engine, die-cast Percy engine, Harold the helicopter, and 4 cargo crates for loading and unloading.

For preschool, train lovers ages 3 years and older.
