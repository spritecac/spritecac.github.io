Encompass is a visual information management platform that accelerates and enhances commercial decisions.

Companies House, Land Registry and a growing list of credit, property and company information providers are available and integrated, empowering even non-expert users to build a rapid understanding of relevant people companies, property and assets from the very beginning of a case. By delivering a fast, easy way to search, review and manage commercial information
