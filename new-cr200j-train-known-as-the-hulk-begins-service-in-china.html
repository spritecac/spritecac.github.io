As part of China’s new train diagram policy, the new CR200J Fuxing EMU train was put into service from Kunming City to Mengzi City in southwest China on January 5. Social media gave it a catchy nickname “The Hulk”. The coach was based of the existing type 25T coach, with a designed speed of 160km/h using a centralized power system. The model will eventually replace regular trains in China’s railway network.
