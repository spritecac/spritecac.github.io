Alex Gaudino began his adventure in the music business in Italy in 1993 at the famous Flying Records and UMM. "Destination Calabria" is the single that elevated Alex to the level of international top djs as Eric Prydz and Fedde le Grand – it reached Number 1 in the European Dance charts in May 2007 and has already achieved the following national chart positions - No.1 in Belgium, No.4 in UK, No.6 in France, No. 10 in Holland
