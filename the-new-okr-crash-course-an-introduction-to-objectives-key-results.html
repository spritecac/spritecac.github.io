OKR (Objectives & Key Results) is a goal setting method and a goal management framework that helps companies turn strategy into measurable and inspiring goals everyone can work toward.

Soon after tech companies like Google, Intel, and LinkedIn started setting OKRs, OKR spread to organizations across all sizes and industries all over the world.
