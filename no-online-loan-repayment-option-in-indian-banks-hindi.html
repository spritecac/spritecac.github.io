Loan repayment online option is not available for most Indian Banks and Financial Institutions like ICICI bank, LIC Housing Finance, Bajaj Finserv etc. If an individual wants to prepay a home loan, personal loan, car loan, loan against property or any other loan, he can only pay overdues. He cannot do the part payment of a loan directly online.

Perhaps other popular banks like SBI, Axis Bank, HDFC Bank, PNB (Punjab National Bank), Yes Bank, Kotak Bank etc. are also not providing online loan payment option.
