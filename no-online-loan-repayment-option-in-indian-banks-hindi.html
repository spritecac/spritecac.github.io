Loan repayment online option is not available for most Indian Banks and Financial Institutions like ICICI bank, LIC Housing Finance, Bajaj Finserv etc. If an individual wants to prepay a home loan, personal loan, car loan, loan against property or any other loan, he can only pay overdues. He cannot do the part payment of a loan directly online.

Perhaps other popular banks like SBI, Axis Bank, HDFC Bank, PNB (Punjab National Bank), Yes Bank, Kotak Bank etc. are also not providing online loan payment option.

Loan repayment online option अधिकांश Indian Banks और Financial Institutions के लिए उपलब्ध नहीं है जैसे- ICICI bank, LIC Housing Finance, Bajaj Finserv etc. यदि कोई व्यक्ति home loan, personal loan, car loan, property के against loan या किसी अन्य loan को prepay करना चाहता है, तो वह केवल overdues pay कर सकता है। वह direct ऑनलाइन, loan का part payment नहीं कर सकता है।

शायद अन्य popular banks जैसे SBI, Axis Bank, HDFC Bank, PNB (Punjab National Bank), Yes Bank, Kotak Bank etc. भी online payment option उपलब्ध नहीं करा रहे हैं।
