YOU CHOOSE book by Pippa Goodhart. This is a great book for indecisive people and helping children discuss new words and learn new vocabulary.

It has lovely pictures by Nick Sharratt exploring all sorts of things habitats, clothes, hobbies and even finding the ideal bed. Works for every age range, because you can choose what to discuss.

This would also be a useful resource for bilingual or multilingual children as an opportunity to expand their vocabulary sets.
