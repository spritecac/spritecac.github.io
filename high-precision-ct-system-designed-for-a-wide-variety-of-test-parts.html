After the in-house development was completed, a standardized high-precision CT system was quickly completed and delivered in two different configurations in August. A customer in Israel received one variant with a granite manipulator, the other with a high-precision steel manipulator went to France.

Precision is guaranteed by integrating and monitoring devices such as cooling systems, temperature and humidity controls – even the manipulator itself can be provided in a variety of suitable materials. One system has a manipulator made of steel, the other one is based on granite with very low thermal expansion.

Read more about this in our press release: https://visiconsult.de/hpct/
