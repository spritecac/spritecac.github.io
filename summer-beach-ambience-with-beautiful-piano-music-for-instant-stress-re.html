🌿🌼BENEFITS of NATURE 🌼🌿

Nature brings a sense of peace and tranquility. The sounds, smells, & beauty of nature can help relax your mind, body and soul. Spending time in nature is good for your physical and mental health.

Research reveals that being in nature, or even viewing scenes of nature, reduces anger, fear, and stress and increases pleasant feelings. Natural sounds improve health, increase positive affect, and lower stress and annoyance. Natural sounds alone – such as waterfalls and birdsong – are good for our health.
