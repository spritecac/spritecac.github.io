A SELF-CONFESSED tanning addict has revealed that he uses tanning injections daily to achieve his sun-kissed complexion. Jimmy Featherstone, 22, from Hull, UK, claims he has been regularly using sunbeds and fake tan products since he was as young as 15 years old. Nowadays, his morning routine includes applying five layers of tan, and he says he gets through a bottle of fake tan every week. For the past year, Jimmy has even been using tanning injections. Determined to become the next Human Ken Doll, he has already spent £10,000 on fillers and veneers and is planning to soon undergo his first major operation - a nose job. Jimmy’s quest to look ‘plastic and tantastic’ has raised concerns among his family and friends who think he’s going too far…
