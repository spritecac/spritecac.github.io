Nowadays, his morning routine includes applying five layers of tan, and he says he gets through a bottle of fake tan every week. For the past year, Jimmy has even been using tanning injections. Determined to become the next Human Ken Doll, he has already spent £10,000 on fillers and veneers and is planning to soon undergo his first major operation - a nose job.
