A SELF-CONFESSED tanning addict has revealed that he uses tanning injections daily to achieve his sun-kissed complexion. Nowadays, his morning routine includes applying five layers of tan, and he says he gets through a bottle of fake tan every week. For the past year, Jimmy has even been using tanning injections.
