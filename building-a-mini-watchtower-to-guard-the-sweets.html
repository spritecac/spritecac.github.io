So, our tower will be smaller than the real one. But still, it will be fun! The watchtower is based on a special sheet. There are spots, where we’ll place the tower itself and its walls. Next, we assemble a special paper construction. So to speak, the prototype of our tower. Then comes the time of the most interesting part! We’re putting mini bricks on the watchtower.
