Learn how to pronounce the English words MAN, MEN, WOMAN, WOMEN correctly with this American English pronunciation lesson.
Man is pronounced /mæn/ and rhymes with can, pan, fan, ran.
Men is pronounced /mɛn/ and rhymes with den, hen, pen.
Woman is pronounced /wʊmən/.
Women is pronounced /wɪmɪn/.
