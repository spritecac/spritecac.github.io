This video documents a training program to assist Vanuatu’s village-level climate change and resource
management champions to fulfill their aspirations by learning from existing and successful community-based
initiatives in a peer-to-peer learning format.
Funded by the ADB-Coral Triangle Initiative in collaboration with ANZDEC, the program provided ni-Vanuatu participants with mentoring and onsite training by experts from SPC-GIZ, Vanuatu’s award-winning Nguna-Pele Marine & Land Protected Area Network, the Government of Vanuatu, and Island Reach.
