If you’re just starting to create your first digital marketing strategy or you’ve been using a strategy but it hasn’t been getting great results, it might be worth standing back and reviewing just who it is you’re trying to market your products or services to and how you do it.

In this video, I’ll take you through the six main questions I suggest you ask before you create your next digital strategy, including:

1) How old are they?
2) Where do they live?
3) Which is the most common gender?
4) What’s the best promotion style?
5) Where do they spend their time?
6) Which is the best type of content for them?
