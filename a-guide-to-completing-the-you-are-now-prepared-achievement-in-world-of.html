In this video we guide you on how to complete the You Are Now Prepared! Achievement that is gotten by completing the Argus campaign. The You Are Now Prepared! Achievement is required to unlock both the Void ElF and the Lightforged Draenei Allied Races on the Alliance Side. Once you have completed the You are Now Prepared! Achievement you’ll get access to the Lightforged Draenei and Void Elf unlock quest. This complete guide will show you from beginning to end of the Argus campaign, even how to start the Argus campaign and get the You are Now Prepared Achievement.
