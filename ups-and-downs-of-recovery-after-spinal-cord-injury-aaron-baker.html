Find out how Aaron Baker started walking again after a profoundly life-altering accident.

Aaron Baker is a motocross rider who was diagnosed with an indefinite quadriplegic after a riding accident in 1999. Despite only being given a 1 in a million chance of even being able to feed himself again, Aaron has fought every step of the way to walk, get back on a bike and maybe even ski. Gain insight into the ups and downs of Aaron’s recovery and the hopes he has that one day he might make a full recovery with the help of the latest medical research.
