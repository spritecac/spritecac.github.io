There is still no sign of Brian Laundrie, the person of interest in Gabby Petito’s death, but authorities are not giving up and returned to the Carlton Reserve for the fifth day.

North Port police haven’t revealed why they’re continuing the search in this massive nature preserve other than to say that he’s still missing and this is where is parents said he last went.
