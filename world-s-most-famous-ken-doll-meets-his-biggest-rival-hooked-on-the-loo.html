WELCOME to the Million Dollar Body Club. Known worldwide as the “Human Ken Doll,” host Justin Jedlica has spent over $1m to achieve plastic perfection - designing some of his own custom implants and becoming a plastic surgery consultant in the process. Today, Justin is joined poolside by plastic connoisseur, PoisonEyez. Justin told Truly: “I’m excited to see PoisonEyez. He stands out a lot for me because he’s had a lot of super high end procedures. He is the ultimate high end LA plastic surgery junkie and he might give me a run for my money…” PoisonEyez added: “I’ve been cut, pulled, pumped, sucked and stitched back up by the best plastic surgeons in the world." Justin will learn more about Poison’s body transformation and how much he has spent on his look. However, will there be any rivalry between these two Ken Dolls?
