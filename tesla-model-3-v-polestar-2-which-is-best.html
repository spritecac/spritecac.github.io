If you’re in the market for a high-quality yet affordable EV, which should you go for - the Polestar 2, or the Tesla Model 3? Well, we’re here to help you choose!

Starting with the Tesla Model 3, it’s one of the most recognisable EVs on our roads today. This entry-level Standard Range Plus edition is powered by a single motor that drives the rear wheels to deliver 296hp & 376Nm of torque. It has a claimed range of 278 miles and costs around £42,000.

Now the Polestar 2 may be a much rarer find on our roads, but we think it can easily match up to the Tesla! As for the single motor edition, there are two versions available, with the range-topper coming equipped with a 75kWh battery that can put down 231hp & 330Nm of torque. Its starting price is around £40,000.

So, which do you think will come out on top? You’ll have to stick with Mat to find out for yourself!
