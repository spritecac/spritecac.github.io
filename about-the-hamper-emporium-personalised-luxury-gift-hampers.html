The Hamper Emporium are Australia’s leaders in gourmet gift hampers. For over a decade we’ve developed partnerships with world-class brands to bring the finest luxury gift hampers and personalised gift hampers to ensure we lead the way with value, quality and elegance with over 200,000 happy customers and counting across Australia.

Our customers are the heart of our business, and we’ve spent years refining every part of The Hamper Emporium experience. From seamless ordering, luxurious custom-designed packaging, personalised gift hampers, and of course sourcing only the best of the best suppliers.

Our attention to detail ensures that every recipient receives a gift hamper that looks amazing, from the outer packaging to the custom-designed luxury gift boxes packed full of the most exquisite brands, flavours and gifts.

Proudly Australian-owned, The Hamper Emporium was founded by owner Emily McWaters in 2007. As a passionate entrepreneur, Emily used her experience as a gourmet food wholesaler to develop strong partnerships with leading brands and fill a gap in the Australian gift hamper market. From humble beginnings that began with Emily standing solo in shopping centres to gather sales, to a proud Sydney based team of passionate and talented staff that continues to grow year on year. The Hamper Emporium is an Australian business success story and we look forward to delivering happiness on your behalf to someone special.

For more information and gift inspiration, visit https://www.thehamperemporium.com.au/
