The "Music-Key" project was a Youth Exchange Program in which individuals from six countries (Belgium, Bulgaria, Germany, Greece, Italy and UK) took part. Both visual impaired and sighted people created a choir performance at the end of the project on 30th of June, 2018. KEAT, PST and Career Sign were the Greek organisations that collaborate in this project.

We would like to thank all the participants, the management team, the volunteers and especially Katia Molfessi.
