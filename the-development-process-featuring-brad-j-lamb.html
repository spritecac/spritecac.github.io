Brad J Lamb, founder of Lamb Development Corporation answers these questions and more in this instalment of the Did You Know video series by Fortress Real Developments.

Mr. Lamb explains the relatively long and complicated development process in just two and half minutes. He does this by breaking down the process into 5 steps providing his unique insights as both a developer and real estate broker.
