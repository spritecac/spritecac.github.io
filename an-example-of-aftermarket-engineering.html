Episode 74: Vince Barbarie is the Senior Director of Engineering, and Bill Mirth is the Director of Sales at IPD (https://www.ipdparts.com/). In this episode, we also welcome back Bill Mirth, who was our guest way back in episode 10 (https://heavydutypartsreport.com/10-ipd/).
