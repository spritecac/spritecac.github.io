Identical twin brothers Darik and Dylan finally reunite a piano duet that they played 10 years ago when they said goodbye to their parents for 2 full years, and went on a church mission in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Our parents always get emotional when we play this song together because I think it reminds them of how hard it was to say goodbye, but also of all the memories we shared through email during those 2 years and the amazing adventure and work that it was. We didn’t see each other for 18 months, although we were hours apart, and when we did it was for a meeting with a lot of people and they had us play this duet for them as well. Dylan was supposed to play a piano solo in church this last week, but then we surprised my parents half way through and played this again for them. When we looked out at them in the audience they both were crying like little children haha! Especially Daddy Rick. That man sure knows how to bawl and show his soft side. Love them so much!
