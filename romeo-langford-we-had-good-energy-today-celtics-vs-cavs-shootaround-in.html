CLEVELAND, OH, – Romeo Langford spoke to the media on Monday ahead of their rematch with the Cleveland Cavaliers. "We had good energy today," Romeo Langford told reporters after shootaround ahead the Celtics’ rematch vs. the Cavs. "We weren’t dwelling on [Saturday’s] loss. We all know what happened, what we needed to work on, and we just came in to try to get better."
