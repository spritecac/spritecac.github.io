This video is about "On the contrary" .
It is used for the given situation:
Ex: Wo bhola nahi hai bulki chalak hai.
Maine usko thappad nahi mara bulki usne mujhe thappad mara.

RULE[Negative sentence + on the contrary + sentence]
Ex: He is not innocent; on the contrary, he is a clever boy.
I didn’t slap him; on the contrary, he slapped me.
