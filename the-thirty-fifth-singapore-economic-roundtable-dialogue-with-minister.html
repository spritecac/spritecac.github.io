The SER is a biannual meeting of private sector economists, business leaders and policymakers. Participants discuss and debate major macroeconomic policy issues facing the domestic economy at the meeting, which includes a Special Session focusing on a specific contemporary topic of economic interest. The topic of the Special Session for the 35th SER will be “Fiscal Policy and Policy Measures”.
