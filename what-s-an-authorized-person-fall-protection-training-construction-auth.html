What’s an Authorized Person? Fall Protection Training: Construction Authorized Person OSHA 2121

Falling from heights can be extremely dangerous, and sometimes even fatal. In this safety training video we will teach you about the authorized person’s roles and responsibilities, and how they can help prevent falls on construction sites.

Safety is our #1 priority here at Arthur and Hansen, and we designed this series exclusively for the construction industry. We know that OSHA doesn’t always put things in the clearest terms, so we wanted to make it easy to understand for everyone.

In this Tailgate-Safety-Topic we talk about one of the most important roles in a fall prevention program, specifically about the authorized person and how they can make sure your workers are as safe as possible when working from heights. More than 1000 people die every year due to falls from heights on construction sites, and in this video we teach you how to prevent falls and improve your fall prevention program.
