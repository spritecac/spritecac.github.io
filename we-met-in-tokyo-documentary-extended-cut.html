“We Met in Tokyo” is the title of the debut EP by Substantial and Funky DL. “We Met in Tokyo" documentary is a film where Funky DL and Substantial reflect on how they first met after working with the late Japanese producer, Nujabes. The two veteran emcees discuss their individual careers, the music they’ve created, and their latest project. The extended version features an additional 20+ minutes of interview footage.
