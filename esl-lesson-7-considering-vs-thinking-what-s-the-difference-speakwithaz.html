In this video, I will talk about the difference between Considering and Thinking. How can we use these similar words and when can we use them? Let’s clear out these concepts!

Considering: We consider important things. This means that this decision will require serious thinking and there will be a lag time until the action is completed.

Thinking: We think trivial things. Like everyday activity. The time from the moment an idea enters your head to the time when the action is completed is very short.
