About this video:
To continue from #Part1, this is the #Part2 of the underground construction. In this video I would like to show you guys about the new update on the last awesome underground house. This is the unique and most creative idea to build such a great underground swimming pool ever. I still dig the soil to build this amazing construction.
There was an unbelievable situation that I never met before. While my friend and I was digging the soil, we saw a really big python in a huge hole with a lot of eggs. Suddenly, my friend and I decide to let her free and we forgot that she remained many eggs inside the hole. Actually, we try to find that big python and give her the eggs, but we can’t. So we decide to cook and eat those eggs in order to survive. After that we got more power to continue our hard work to dig the underground swimming pool.
We got a lot of questions from the views and other friends, but one of those is “How can we maintain the water in the soil like that? It must leak.” That is a very common question. We got our own technique to do it. We need to find the termite house in the forest to mix with the red soil to make it more sticky. And then we use it to cover the bottom of the pool.
It’s such a hard work for us because it took almost three weeks to finish this project.
