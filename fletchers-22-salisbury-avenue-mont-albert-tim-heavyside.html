Enviably close to lovely Hamilton Street cafes and shops, Mont Albert train station and the 109 tram, this warmly inviting 1920s double brick home represents a truly exciting opportunity for families.

A superbly sun-filled design which has been extended over the years with great success, the generous four bedroom, two bathroom plus home office configuration offers perfect dimensions with potential to enhance further if desired.

Beyond the light filled enclosed entry verandah, soaring ceilings, leadlight, open fireplaces and bay windows decorate the lounge and separate dining room. Matched with an open-plan meals/family room, a bright kitchen with Bosch appliances and a large alfresco deck, lush garden views fill every space.

On a vast 936 sq m approx. block with redevelopment potential (STCA), families will flourish in this tree-lined pocket just off Mont Albert Road and zoned for Mont Albert Primary School. Close to Box Hill Central, TAFE and hospital, enjoy easy access to Melbourne’s finest schools.
