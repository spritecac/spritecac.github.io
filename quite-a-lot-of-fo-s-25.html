Rules For The Accessory MAL 2021
Eligibility:
Our only request is that your project is a wearable accessory
All items made of yarn or fiber are welcome: spinning, crochet, knitting, weaving.
Works in progress are welcome if no more than 50% of the project has been completed by the beginning of 2021.
Projects that are included in any other make-along are welcome (Multiple dipping is encouraged).

To Enter:
This make-along will use Ravelry. There will be Finished Objects (no chatter) and Chatter threads in both the Yarn Tales by The Sea and the Not Quite Enough Yarn groups. To be eligible for entry, please become a member of these groups.

You are very warmly invited to post in both the Yarn Tales By the Sea and the Not Quite Enough Yarn threads – double-dip for twice the chance of winning!

Finished Objects:
Post a picture of your finished object in the FO thread. One post per project. This is a no-chatter thread, but by all means add a bit of description, especially if the ocean connection isn’t obvious (eg it’s the pattern name).

Chatter Thread:
Post anything to do with the MAL, comment on the projects of others, share your thoughts and be part of the community.

Prizes:
All Prizes will be drawn using Random Number Generator
Chatter Thread: Every quarter a pattern prize (available on Ravelry) will be awarded, to a maximum value of $10 (US), and will be given online.

Finished Object Thread: This will be a physical prize (eg yarn), which will be sent by post.

To claim your prize:
The prize winners will be announced on the relevant podcast. This will be the only notification. The winner must get in contact before the 25th of the month following the podcast to claim their prize. If there is no contact by this time, the prize will be re-drawn.

Any questions don’t hesitate to email me at Yarntales.bythesea@gmail.com

Thanks for your interest and good
