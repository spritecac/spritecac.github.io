Good Morning Vogue, Vogue’s new fashion news show, was conceived in response to this tumultuous year. Fashion needs to change, and some of the industry’s biggest leaders are speaking about it. Designer Kerby Jean-Raymond has a candid conversation about his next move, his new project Friends in New York and why in fashion, “the old system just doesn’t…work.” Céline Semaan and other activists about fashion’s responsibility in creating a new culture around climate change, diversity, inclusion, and beyond.
