Raising SSI benefits to the federal poverty line of a $1073 each and every month would be a massive increase and help out millions of Americans. The SSI program otherwise known as supplemental security income supplies monthly benefits to nearly 8,000,000 beneficiaries who are low income adults, seniors, and people with disabilities. The SSI Program is administered by the Social Security administration and could potentially translate into higher benefits for Social Security retirement, Social Security disability, Social Security survivors, SSI and VA benefits.
