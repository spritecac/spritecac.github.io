In this video, we discuss some great new updates on the $1073 Raise / Increase to SSI benefits as a result of the SSI restoration act. This is some great updates as there are millions of Americans receiving SSI benefits each and every month who need a Raise to Benefits as soon as possible. This is in reference to SSI otherwise known as supplemental security income benefits and the plan to raise these benefits to $1073 which is the federal poverty line. Raising SSI benefits to the federal poverty line of a $1073 each and every month would be a massive increase and help out millions of Americans. The SSI program otherwise known as supplemental security income supplies monthly benefits to nearly 8,000,000 beneficiaries who are low income adults, seniors, and people with disabilities. The SSI Program is administered by the Social Security administration and could potentially translate into higher benefits for Social Security retirement, Social Security disability, Social Security survivors, SSI and VA benefits.
