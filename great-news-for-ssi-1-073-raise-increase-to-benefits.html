Raising SSI benefits to the federal poverty line of a $1073 each and every month would be a massive increase and help out millions of Americans. The SSI Program is administered by the Social Security administration and could potentially translate into higher benefits for Social Security retirement, Social Security disability, Social Security survivors, SSI and VA benefits.
