Kylie Minogue and Jason Donovan’s Christmas 1988 single “Especially For You” actually hit the top spot in the UK on 1st January 1989 and went on to sell a million copies in that territory alone. It remains producers Stock Aitken and Waterman’s biggest seller in Britain and Kylie’s second biggest behind “Can’t Get You Out Of My Head”. The track also made the top 10 in their native Australia, Belgium, Denmark, the Netherlands, Finland, France, Germany, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway and Switzerland.
