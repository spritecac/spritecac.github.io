David Bell, a former Principal of Trinity Methodist Theological College and active Methodist minister has created this series of stand-alone video talks for the Practical Theology Channel and kiwiconnexion.nz from the Trinity-at-Waiake eLearning Centre.

kiwiconnexion.nz has an educational resourcing Live-on-air Night School for ministers and lay people who want continuing best practices for practical theology. You can view the recorded event without needing to login
