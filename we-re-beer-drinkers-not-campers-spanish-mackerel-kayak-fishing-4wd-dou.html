It didn’t take our staff much convincing…
We were all ITCHING to get up the beach for a weekend of mateship and memories that will last a lifetime.
We packed our bags and headed up the beach to our favourite spot on the Sunshine Coast…Double Island Point.

Day one was spent on the Teewah beach side of Double Island. The Larry’s were flowing and Hep managed to catch a nice, big Spanish Mackarel that fed the whole camp!

Day two was spent on the Rainbow Beach side - Fishing around the Double Island Point Headland, relaxing by the Rainbow Beach lagoon and drinking Your Mates beers with our team!
