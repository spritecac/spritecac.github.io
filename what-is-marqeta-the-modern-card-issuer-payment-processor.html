What is Marqeta? The Modern Card Issuer & Payment Processor. How does Marqeta work? What does Marqeta do? When Will Marqeta IPO? Who is Jason Gardner? Marqeta is a modern card issuer and payment processing business founded in 2010 that is due to IPO in 2021, potentially listing with a valuation in excess of $15 billion. Jason Gardner founded the business 11 years ago to revolutionise the payments industry and bring credit card issuance, banking relationships, fraud prevention and deep insights all under one roof. Their client base now includes Klarna, Uber, Instacart, DoorDash, Kabbage and many more. But How did Marqeta grow into a unicorn business and what is the story of Marqeta?
