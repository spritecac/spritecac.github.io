Did you know that in Gmail it is possible to give someone else access to your inbox? Very useful if you want to create a shared mailbox or for a PA looking to manage an executive’s emails and calendar. With #DelegatedAccess in #Gmail you can do exactly that. Now with up to 1000 users.

In this video Damson Cloud’s Fintan Murphy covers:

1. What exactly are delegated mailboxes?
2. The recent increases in the number of concurrent users that can access a delegated mailbox.
3. A quick demo on how to set up a delegated mailbox.
