Join us for a livestream of our 11.30am service from our warehouse in Watford! Our services are led by Mike, Andy & members of our team and we’d love you to join us as we worship Jesus, hear from God’s word, and make space to pray and see how God wants to meet with us. This Sunday we’ll be continuing our series on the ministry of the Holy Spirit.

If you’d like to join us for our in-person services come along to our warehouse in Watford at 9.15am, 11.30am, 4pm or 6.30pm on any Sunday!
