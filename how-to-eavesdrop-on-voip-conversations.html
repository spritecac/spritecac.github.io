Warning: To avoid prosecution, it is recommended that you only perform this attack on your own network and VoIP system. Eavesdropping is a federal crime under the Wiretap Act and Electronic Communications Privacy Act. If you record any incoming or outgoing communications, you should inform the recipient or caller that he or she is being recorded.
