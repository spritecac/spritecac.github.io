Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. CYPRUS. USA.
Stefanovikeio airport is "on fire" in recent days, as the work carried out there has been intensified, in order to prepare the space for the reception and hospitality of the US forces, within the framework of the Atlantic Resolve.
So, as we can see in the photos published by US side, a few days ago, officers of the Hellenic and US forces were there, to see and inspect the area and to jointly plan their next moves.
In fact, the accompanying text states that the concrete pad of the airfield was recently completed, so that to be ready for the arrival of the US team of helicopters.
It is also pointed out that the Apache and Kiowa helicopters of the Hellenic Air Force are preparing to conduct exercises with the US.
Of course, we do not yet know how many helicopters will arrive, but judging by the preparations, they will probably be many and will certainly irritate the Turkish side even more, which lately, has been unable to understand where the blows it receives are coming from.
