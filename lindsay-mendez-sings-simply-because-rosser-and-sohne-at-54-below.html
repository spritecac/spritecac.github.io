From "Run Away Home": Run Away Home tells the story of a family whose youngest child, Austin, has been missing for three years. LeAnne Parker and her daughter Amber are still struggling with the aftermath of this loss when a boy claiming to be Austin shows up in town. Though he looks different than the child they remember, LeAnne believes him, creating a rift with her more skeptical daughter. Part thriller, part psychological drama, Run Away Home is about the power of hope, the dangers of self-deception and the consequences of bringing long buried secrets to light.
