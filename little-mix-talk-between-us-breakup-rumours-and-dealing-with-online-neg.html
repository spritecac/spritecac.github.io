Girl group Little Mix are celebrating 10 years together with their greatest hits album ‘Between Us’. The girls talk about their favourite new tracks from the album, the importance of wanting to inspire, and represent female empowerment. As well, Little Mix address the breakup rumours, dealing with online negativity, their newborn children, their love for "Canada’s Drag Race" and their hopes for the future of Little Mix.
