Don’t let the simplicity of this spell fool you. If you need a specific amount of money, give this a try!

Ingredients:
1. Piece of paper (any color or size)
2. Pen or pencil (any color)
3. Green or gold candle.

00:00:00 - Introduction
00:00:30 - On the piece of paper write the following verse from Psalm 35:
"Let the Lord be magnified which hath pleasure in the prosperity of his servant."
00:00:48 - Under the verse, write the specific amount of money needed, plus the words, "this sum or even greater."
00:01:06 - Sign your name.
00:01:22 - Place the candle on top of the paper and light it.

00:01:44 - Memorize the verse and repeat it throughout the day. Try to get at least 30 repetitions of the verse in during the day. Repeat it as you go to sleep, and as soon as you remember to in the morning.

00:02:48 - Hide the paper in your home. Once the money has manifested, rip it up and throw it away.
