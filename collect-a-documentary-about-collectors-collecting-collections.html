"Collect" - A Documentary about Collectors, Collecting, and Collections

What drives someone to collect something? Is it a fascination, love or an uncontrollable desire? In this documentary, we’ll explore people’s various collections and find out what started their insatiable drive. Whether it be Funko Pops, Disney pins, dolls, or rocks! Will their collections ever be complete? What’s the endgame? These are some of the questions we hope to answer. Join us and meet the sources… the collector’s themselves.
