A Colorado woman whose contractor destroyed a tile shower over a payment dispute is speaking out about the incident and showing off her newly-renovated bathroom to Inside Edition. The now-viral video shows contractor Terry Gregory taking a sledgehammer to the walls and tile floor after he says Amber Trucke refused to pay him fully for his work. Several contractors were so upset about the video, they banded together to give Trucke the bathroom of her dreams, completely free of charge.
