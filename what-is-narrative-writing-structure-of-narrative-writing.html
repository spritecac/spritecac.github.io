Get to know what Narrative Writing is, in this video.
—
A Narrative is a writing that tells a story. It can be written to motivate entertain or to educate the reader. Narratives can be both fact and fiction. A Narrative writing includes Characters, Setting, Conflict, Climax and Resolution.
