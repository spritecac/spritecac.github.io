Tonight we are test fitting a tenon for the planer stand before we go ahead and cut all of the mortises. This one went together fairly well without too much adjustment to the tenon. I can tell you that smaller tenons are fussier than timber framing tenons, mistakes show up much easier. That usually is true of most things involved with woodworking. I am hoping that by the end of the weekend we will have most of this thing wrapped up and ready to put it together. This project has been a nice change from the insulation videos and I am enjoying it very much.
