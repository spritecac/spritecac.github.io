Right where you need it

The IB-HUB1408-CR is a clamp hub that allows you to place the most important interfaces for your storage media and devices exactly where you need them.

Suitable for monitors without heavy profiled edges. Alternatively, the clamping hub can be attached to a suitably sized table top to take advantage of the easily accessible USB ports.

With two USB 3.0 Type-A and one USB 3.0 Type-C™ ports as well as SD and microSD card readers, we have everything you need for everyday you use within easy reach. The card readers are multi-LUN compatible, allowing both memory cards to be used at the same time.
