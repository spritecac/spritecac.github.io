In the latest edition of ThePrintOTC, author Chetan Bhagat sat down with the ThePrint’s Editor-in-Chief Shekhar Gupta to talk about his upcoming book — 400 Days — and how he tackled the challenge of writing a thriller and mystery novel, the challenges of making people read books today, and why curation of content is the future. Bhagat also spoke passionately against Literature Festivals, explaining why he had a problem with them, and how people who say Chetan Bhagat doesn’t count, make him count.
