The air quality in the greater area of Jakarta, Indonesia is the worst in South East Asia.

Continuously breathing in Jakarta’s air could cut the residents’ average life expectancy by up to five years.

A group of residents sued the government over the matter in 2019. After more than two years, they have finally received a verdict.
