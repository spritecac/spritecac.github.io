The Auburn - Entertain in luxury
http://asth.com.au/homes/auburn

Welcome to the charming Allsteel Auburn Display Home. Come with us, as we take you on a tour of this vibrant home, perfectly suited to the avid entertainer. Featuring three bedrooms plus office and home theatre, there’s plenty of space to enjoy.

As we enter through the stunning foyer with its unique feature wall, we come to the spacious home office, filled with beautiful natural light through its large windows. This is the perfect place to catch up on emails or even work from home!

The open plan living area features sophisticated dining adjacent to a cosy lounge, with stunning fireplace.

A purpose-built home theatre offers the ultimate in comfort, with thick, quality carpet and floor to ceiling curtains.

The beautiful kitchen is ideally located at the heart of the home, and boasts clever features such as soft close drawers and quality stainless steel appliances neatly tucked away, all overlooking the breathtaking alfresco.

Down the hall, are the restful bedrooms, featuring ample space to relax and unwind. Plus practical and stylish built in wardrobes.

Bedroom number two across the hall, is the perfect room for the growing child, with a handy walk-in robe to help keep toys and clothes neatly organised.

The master bedroom is the essence of tranquillity, with large double doors opening to the front verandah, and a cleverly tucked away ensuite bathroom.

Across the hall is the main bathroom, featuring an enormous full-width mirror over a stylish vanity, as well as a stunning freestanding bath, making it the perfect place to relax.

A handy laundry at the end of the hall opens to the back deck area, for maximum practicality and efficiency.

The rear verandah is a delightful addition to the home, flowing into a spacious alfresco area ideally suited to entertaining. An outdoor kitchen provides the perfect place to host gatherings on warm summer evenings, or cosy winter nights.

The beautiful Auburn will leave you amazed by its distinguished elegance and superb layout.
