ADM Energy’s (LON:ADME) Osamede Okhomina introduces their new chief engineer to Proactive London.

Dr Babatunde Pearse will be responsible for planning the next phase of the Aje development and overseeing the FEED (“Front End Engineering Design”) studies.

These will define the project requirements for detailed engineering, procurement and construction of facilities to support the Final Investment Decision.
