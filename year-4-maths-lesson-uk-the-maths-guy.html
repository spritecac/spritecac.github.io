This is your Year 4 Maths Lesson teaching all of the essential skills needed to be successful in Maths.
We start the lesson looking at multi digit addition, then move into subtraction and then division and Multiplication.
These are your essential 4 corners of Maths which you’ll need to have a solid understanding of before moving on to deeper harder things.

After this we will learn about adding and subtracting fractions and numbers that involve decimals before finishing the lesson with understanding how to calculate the perimeter of a polygon.
