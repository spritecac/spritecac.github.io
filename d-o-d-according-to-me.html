“The finest purveyor of Future Jack, D.O.D” returns to his now fixed home of Axtone records, bringing with him a fully road tested banger that represents a shift in his sound, Futurejack 1.5 if you will, which sets the tone for 2019 and FutureJack 2.0.

It’s fair to say that ‘According To Me’ has been D.O.D’s secret weapon over the last few months, a period that saw his own debut event at ADE, a demolition of Bootshaus and taking over from Tiesto at the gargantuan Magnetic festival in Prague.

Dan’s DJ dexterity has not gone unnoticed and 2019 will see his return to the Pioneer studios where he will be recording a fresh mix for Pioneer DJ sounds showcasing the DJs 1000 that looks set to be a central part of his live performance evolution. Coupled with a return to the legendary Box at Ministry of Sound and an ever-expanding diary, expect to see a lot more of him as the year progresses.
