Calcutta has partnered with Broken Bow recording artist James Wesley to produce the single and music video "Hooked Up". "Hooked Up" tells the story of Anglers pursuing Blue Marlin - told through the eyes of a marlin at the Big Rock, a legendary fishing spot off the coast of Morehead City, North Carolina.

A portion of the song downloads benefit the Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament Charities and the ASA’s Keep America Fishing Foundation.
