DANIELA, 22, from Finland describes herself as ‘an old soul stuck in the 80s’. With her ‘bat’s nest’ hair and heavy black eye makeup, the UK-based uni student says she is inspired by her favourite 80s bands and artists. She also loves creating and sharing her unique makeup looks and custom clothing with her 36,000 followers on Instagram. However, in spite of her distinctly gothic look, Daniela says she sees Barbie as a feminist icon and will transform into a Barbie-inspired 90s look. Swapping her deathly pale makeup for fake tan and her black hair for blonde, Daniela’s new look is a complete departure from her signature style…
