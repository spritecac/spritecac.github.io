Hester Peirce - aka Crypto Mom - recently made a speech at the Texas Blockchain Summit and shared her views on the crypto space and the regulatory environment. As one of five SEC Commissioners, she is in a unique position to comment on the space, describing it as the Wild West and Frontier (sound familiar?).

After raising questions about regulatory clarity and investor protection, she ends the speech quoting the Bankless disclaimer and send-off: "We are headed West. This is the frontier. It’s not for everyone…" We deeply appreciate Hester’s work at the center of crypto regulation, and this speech deserves to be heard in full.
