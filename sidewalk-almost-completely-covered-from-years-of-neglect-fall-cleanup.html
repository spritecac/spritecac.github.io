I got this job from one of the ads I posting on facebook trying to help people out. These are some new renters that just moved in and the landlord was supposed to have the yard cleaned up but didn’t. Obviously we said we would come get it taken care of.
There wasn’t much grass on this one but the sidewalk had a ton of dirt that had been washed over it. I would say more than half of the sidewalk was covered. This job took about 2.5 hours to complete start to finish.
