Meticulously maintained and conveniently located, this freestanding EaDo home (with yard space and no HOA) offers a great location for you to call home. Enjoy the convenient location in a gated community in the heart of EaDo, where you can walk to all the patio bars and restaurants! You’re also just steps away from the Columbia Tap Rail Trail, where you can ride your bike all the way down to Brays Bayou! Entertain with your open second floor living, dining, and kitchen area. The kitchen boasts a large breakfast bar and granite island with ample cabinet space and gas cooking. The living room boasts tons of natural light and provides a large space to entertain, complete with a dry-bar space - great for additional storage! The Seller has added some great upgrades to this home, like a water softener / filtration system, low-maintenance crushed rock in the outdoor space, window coverings throughout, security system, and pre-wired with cameras.
