I am enthusiastic about books.

enthusiastic /ɪnˌθuː.ziˈæs.tɪk/: showing enthusiasm:
e.g.
You don’t seem very enthusiastic about the party - don’t you want to go tonight?

enthusiasm /ɪnˈθuː.zi.æz.əm/
a feeling of energetic interest in a particular subject or activity and an eagerness to be involved in it:
e.g.
One of the good things about teaching young children is their enthusiasm.
After the accident he lost his enthusiasm for the sport.
