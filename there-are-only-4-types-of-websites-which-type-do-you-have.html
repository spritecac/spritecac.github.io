Every website can be sorted into one of four types. By knowing what kind you want before making any design or marketing decisions, you can save yourself from a world of hurt and wasted money.

Do you know what kind of website you have or want to have? If you answered “No,” this video is just for you. By identifying what kind of website you need, you are able to save time and money in designing the right website for your company.

For more information, check out our blog post on this topic: https://thrive.design/there-are-4-types-of-websites-which-one-do-you-need/
