A great many classic cars and pre-war vehicles turned up for the Smallwood Vintage Rally, in this video we walk around the car exhibits. A few of the old cars we’d seen before at other classic car shows, but quite a few were new to us. Cars on show included examples of Ford Granada Mk1 saloon and coupe, Lanchester LD10, Singer Gazelle, Ford 100E and various Mk1 Escorts. Pre-war and vintage cars on show included a couple of fabulous Ford Model Ts, a Model Y saloon, a bullnose Morris from the 1920s, and - on sale in the autojumble area - a wonderful old Packard. Rootes Group, Jaguar, BMC & BL were also represented.
This rally also attracts a large number of commercial vehicles and tractors, which will feature in separate videos on this channel shortly (it would have been too long a video to cram everything into this upload). Classic caravans and camper vans also get a mention.
If your classic car is shown here, please pop a note into the comments, equally if you have memories of any car featured it’d be interesting to read your thoughts about them. Videos are being uploaded regularly so please subscribe if you haven’t already.
