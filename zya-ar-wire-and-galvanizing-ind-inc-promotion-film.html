Özyaşar Wire and Galvanizing Ind. Inc. were founded by our honarary president Ahmet Çokyaşar in 1973. Headquarter of Özyaşar is located on Basın Ekspres Road which is 4 kilometers away from Atatürk Airport. The production facilities were constructed in Değirmenköy, Silivri. Total surface is 75.000 squaere meters with 35.000 square-meters indoor area.
