On September 18, 2019, Hantang Culture announces the completion of the restoration of the Emperor’s Box in the Palais Garnier, one of the first fruits of its partnership with the Paris Opera.
The Emperor’s Box is a testimony to the splendid theatre culture from the nineteenth century to the present. Workshops of refined French crafts, such as Manufacture Prelle, Phelippeau Tapissier and MDB (Métiers du Bois), followed Garnier’s original designs closely during the restoration; it took them several months to reverse the passage of almost 150 years.

As 2019 greets the 350th anniversary of the Paris Opera, Hantang Culture offers its unreserved support for this global celebration of the arts.
A special episode of A World of Excellence on the restoration project will be released recently. Follow Hantang Culture to learn more about the preservation of French savoir-faire and cultural heritage.
