Ajax Systems, together with Safe Home Europe security system distributor, launched a pilot project on fire protection of houses and neighborhoods in the Czech Republic. The project is mentored by the Fire Rescue Service of the Czech Republic, and the Czech Association of Fire Officers.

The focus of the project is on the protection of existing multi-storey apartment buildings against fire.
