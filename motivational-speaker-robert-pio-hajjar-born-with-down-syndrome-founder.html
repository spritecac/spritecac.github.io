Robert Pio Hajjar, Founder IDEAL-WAY.ca, opens Best Buddies Regional Conference 2009 in Hamilton, Ontario. Robert, born with Down Syndrome, is the voice for his peers that can not speak. A charismatic, motivational speaker, he travels widely as living proof of what is possible when you include people like him, give them love, encouragement and opportunity. Robert’s signature line is "Keep the faith."
