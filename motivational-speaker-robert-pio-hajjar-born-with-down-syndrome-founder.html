Robert Pio Hajjar, Founder IDEAL-WAY.ca, opens Best Buddies Regional Conference 2009 in Hamilton, Ontario. Robert, born with Down Syndrome, is the voice for his peers that can not speak. Robert’s signature line is "Keep the faith."
