Saad Mohseni, chairman and CEO of Afghanistan’s largest media organisation Moby Group, is our guest at the latest edition of ThePrint ‘Off The Cuff’. In a conversation with Editor-in-Chief Shekhar Gupta, Mohseni spoke about the ongoing political turmoil in Afghanistan and elaborated on the relationship between the Taliban and the Afghans. Mohseni also discussed the activities of and challenges faced by the Moby Group over the years, and what the future holds for media in Afghanistan.
