Biography
Nina Kankunda famously know as “Nina Roz” is a SIinger/Songwriter/ ModelL, born in the capital of kampala in kibuli mutajazi to business woman, Lucy Mbabazi and Hajji Gitta Salongo, Nina was born one, though she has other siblings in a household that was filled with music from day one.
