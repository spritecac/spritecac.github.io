Onerous definition (adjective) involving great effort and difficulty

Onerous pronunciation: on-er-uh s

Examples of Onerous in a sentence:

1. Handling security in a busy nightclub can be an onerous job.

2. While the assignment seems simple, in reality, it is quite onerous.

3. When Jack agreed to help his father cut the grass, he did not realize the chore would be so onerous.

4. Despite his rigid training, the athlete was unprepared to handle the onerous terrain.

5. What can I do to make the move less onerous for you?
