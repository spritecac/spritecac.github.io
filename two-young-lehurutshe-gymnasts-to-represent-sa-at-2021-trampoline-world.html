A pair of young tumblers from Lehurutshe near Zeerust in North West have been selected to represent South Africa at the upcoming 2021 Trampoline Gymnastics World Championships & World Age Group Competition in Baku in Azerbaijan.

The pair are among nine gymnasts who were selected to represent the country in divisions, including trampoline, double mini trampoline and tumbling following a series of gruelling qualifiers.

SABC News reporter Sentleeng Lehihi compiled the report
