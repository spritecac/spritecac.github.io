In this Jim Rohn personal development video, Jim Rohn talks about how to become the master of your mind. This involves becoming a master of your thoughts in order to succeed.

Mastering your mind for success is all about taking charge of the way you think. It’s not just about positive thinking, it’s also about understanding how to use your thoughts and feelings as a tool for change. In this video Jim Rohn shares steps for mastering your mind so that you can be successful in work and life!

Jim rohn is a self-help author and motivational speaker. In this video, he shares his top tips for mastering your mind for success!

1) Focus on the solution not the problem. 2) Identify what you want in life. 3) Be clear with your desires in order to manifest them into reality. 4) Visualize success with positive thoughts and words 5) Use affirmations to boost motivation
