The Mavericks defeated the Bucks, 114-103. Jalen Brunson recorded 17 points, 6 rebounds and 5 assists for the Mavericks, while Giannis Antetokounmpo led all scorers with 26 points (9-11 FG) and 10 rebounds for the Bucks in the losing effort. The Mavericks finish the preseason 4-0, while the Bucks finish 1-4.
