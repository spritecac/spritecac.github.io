‘Waft’ is our sixth short film, directed by Vishnu Udayan. It’s his 7th short film which is so far the biggest project till date. Before the official YoutTube release, we had a festival run where we got selected into 30 International Film Festivals across the globe wherein we also won 14 awards and 10 nominations.

Baffled at the sudden exit of Aradhya from his life, lost -in-love Ashvath has curled into his world of silence, finding refuge in books. A breaking news story fills the missing link and decodes the past but it is probably too late.
