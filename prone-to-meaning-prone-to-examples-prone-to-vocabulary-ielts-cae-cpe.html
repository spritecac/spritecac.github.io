An explanation of the meaning and structure of the adjective prone and the phrase prone to.
If someone is prone to something they have a tendency to do that thing. if someone is prone to something there is a likelihood that they will suffer from this.
Prone can also mean lying down horizontally.
Prone to is a neutral formality structure.
