With winter around the corner, a beautiful and hardworking fireplace is essential. Valor Fireplaces’ new H5 model is compact, stylish and customizable to suit your needs. Choose between birch, driftwood or traditional logs to suit your space and customize the look with a wide selection of liners. Whether it’s striking black glass, traditional red brick or an of-the-moment fluted style, there’s a perfect liner for your unit.
