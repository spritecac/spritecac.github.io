With winter around the corner, a beautiful and hardworking fireplace is essential. Valor Fireplaces’ new H5 model is compact, stylish and customizable to suit your needs. Updated fronts like pewter and aged steel can convey a rustic or contemporary tone. Plus, it’s easy to control the flame size and temperature using your remote or the app!
