With winter around the corner, a beautiful and hardworking fireplace is essential. Valor Fireplaces’ new H5 model is compact, stylish and customizable to suit your needs. Choose between birch, driftwood or traditional logs to suit your space and customize the look with a wide selection of liners. Updated fronts like pewter and aged steel can convey a rustic or contemporary tone. Plus, it’s easy to control the flame size and temperature using your remote or the app!
