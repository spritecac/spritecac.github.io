So this video is a bit longer than my other videos but my sister and I talk about our stats and extracurricular activities that played a part in getting into our respective schools!

We talk about what our SAT/ACT scores were, what sports we were involved in, what clubs we were a part of, what kind of volunteering we had done, and also some advice in order to, hopefully, decrease your stress about applying to colleges!
