An Australian cardiologist has been able to examine a patient’s heart before using his scalpel, after a 3D printer was used to create an exact model of the organ. Researchers at the Victor Chang Cardiac Research Institute, together with doctors at St Vincent’s Hospital, are hoping to one day use human tissue.
