Mattafix are from UK duo along the names of Marlon Roudette and Preetesh Hirji. Their music is a fusion of hip hop/rap, R&B, reggae, dancehall, blues, jazz, soul and world. They won the Sopot International Song Festival in 2006. "To & Fro" is a single from Mattafix’s debut album, Signs of a Struggle, released in 2005. The song features backing vocals from former Sugababe, Siobhán Donaghy. It was released as a download only single on March 13, 2006 in the United Kingdom. Copyright Mattafix
