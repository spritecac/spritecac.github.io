This week, we visit one of the most stunning transformations so far - from an old Ang Mo Kio HDB, to a vibrant and colourful home. It’s a 5-room HDB at a size of 1,324 square feet, which is definitely more spacious than your average HDB home. Homeowners Janan and Sherry chose a colour theme that is very interestingly inspired by their little poodle. The combination of penelope pink and moss green is something that is very striking, but yet complements each other very well.

There are also areas of the house that are inspired by Janan’s mother and grandmother. One of which is the balcony, where Peranakan tiles have been laid out that provides a slightly more retro look. He also has an open pantry, where all the sauces and condiments are very neatly laid out.

The kitchen is also an open concept one, and they also have a wet kitchen, to cater to their private dining concept at home.

Special thanks to Janan and Sherry for taking the time to show us around their lovely home!
