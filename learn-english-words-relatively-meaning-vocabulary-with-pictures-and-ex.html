What does Relatively mean? Learn the meaning of Relatively as we define this advanced vocabulary word with a simple definition, pictures, example sentences, English pronunciation and audio.

Relatively definition (adverb) to a certain degree or in comparison to another thing

Relatively pronunciation: rel-uh-tiv-lee

Examples of Relatively in a sentence:

1. Since the building was built two years ago, it is still relatively new.

2. The debate has been relatively quiet with just a couple of outbursts.

3. Compared to the cost of living in other countries, it’s relatively cheap to live in Costa Rica.

4. At seventeen, Gail is relatively young to be a mother.

5. In comparison to the rundown hotel we stayed in last night, this place is relatively charming.
