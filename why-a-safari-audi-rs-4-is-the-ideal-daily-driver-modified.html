In this week’s episode of MODIFIED, Matt Farah gets behind the wheel of a JDM B5 Audi RS 4 that’s received a Safari conversion courtesy of custom shop AI Design. Matt learns more about what went into this build (like a dog bed!) and, after getting some mud on the tires, concludes that a customized rally wagon can also legitimately double as a practical daily driver.
