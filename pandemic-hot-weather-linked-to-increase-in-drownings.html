Preliminary data from the Lifesaving Society shows more drownings this year than last, particularly in B.C., Alberta, Manitoba and Quebec, and experts say it’s likely a combination of wanting to cool off during the hot weather and finding safe, outdoor activities because of the COVID-19 pandemic.
