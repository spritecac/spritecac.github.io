With funding from HELPAGE CANADA, the Jimmy Pratt Memorial Outreach Centre embarked on a food hamper program to help seniors facing the COVID19 Pandemic. Volunteers load the hampers with non-perishable food items along with some fruit which are then delivered to various residences by taxi. Jane and her children Sam and Katie Ann Henderson along with Melissa and Peter Elliott, Blake Cryderman and Reg Rumsey made this ongoing project a success.
