#Plausible #mywordbook

Meaning of Plausible: seeming reasonable or probable

Examples:
1. our business proposal would be plausible if you actually had five million dollars in the bank.
2. His story sounded perfectly plausible.
3. This explanation fits the facts and is psychologically plausible.
4. Their argument sounds plausible but is it really valid?
5. He was so plausible that he conned everybody.
