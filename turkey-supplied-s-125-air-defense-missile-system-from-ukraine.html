Ukrspecexport General Manager Vadym NOZDRIA announced that Turkey has procured the S-125 Air Defense Missile System from Ukraine. In the panel moderated by BAYKAR Defense General Manager Haluk Bayraktar; Ukraine’s Ambassador to Ankara Vasyl Bodnar, Defense Technology LLC Commercial Director Pavlo Bukin, Ukrspecexport General Manager Vadym Nozdrya and STM General Manager Özgür Güleryüz participated. Referring to the systems supplied by Turkey from Ukraine during the panel, Ukrspecexport General Manager Vadym NOZDRIA announced that Turkey has procured the S-125 Air Defense Missile System from Ukraine. According to the information obtained by SavunmaSanayiST.com, the system, which was delivered to the Turkish user in September of last year, was supplied within the scope of urgent need.
