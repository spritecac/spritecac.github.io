Giannis Antetokounmpo recorded 31 points, 20 rebounds, 5 assists, 2 steals and 3 blocks in 30 minutes of play for the Bucks as they defeated the Magic, 117-108. This is Antetokounmpo’s 3rd career game with 30+ points and 20+ rebounds. Jalen Suggs tallied 14 points, 6 rebounds and 4 assists for the Magic in the losing effort. The Bucks improve to 9-8 on the season, while the Magic fall to 4-13.
