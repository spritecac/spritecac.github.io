Giannis Antetokounmpo recorded 31 points, 20 rebounds, 5 assists, 2 steals and 3 blocks in 30 minutes of play for the Bucks as they defeated the Magic, 117-108. The Bucks improve to 9-8 on the season, while the Magic fall to 4-13.
