One of the greatest fatalities to success is pre-occupation, lack of concentration. If you are unable to concentrate on thing at hand, you will always wind up average. Like any other skill, focused concentration can be learned, and with practice you can get good at it. Start small, get your mind to focus on one activity for a few minutes. Keep repeating this, and gradually increase this time duration. The more you train your mind to concentrate, the easier it will become for you to focus on one thing.
