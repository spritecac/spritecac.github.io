Even even if and even though භාවිතය ගැන සරලව සියල්ල සිංහලෙන් නොමිලේම ඉගෙන ගන්න. Learn to use even, even if and even though in Sinhala.
Even is an adverb and we can observe two meanings in sinhala corresponding to the adverb ‘even’

On the other hand even though and even if are conjunctions which connect sentences. Although they seem similar, they have a distinct difference.
