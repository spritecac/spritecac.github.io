Microsoft just unveiled its new Surface Laptop Studio. It’s a successor to the detachable-screen surface book and features a new hinge, improved processors, and a 120Hz refresh rate display. Pricing starts at $1,599 and includes 16GB of RAM, an 11th gen Intel Core i5 processor with Iris Xe graphics, and 256GB of SSD storage.
