1) What does the New AUKUS Alliance mean for Southeast Asia?
2) Will ASEAN stay neutral or get pulled into China-US growing tensions?
3) What security issues Indo-Pacific region will face?
4) Will AUKUS be able to contain China?

The Quad and the AUKUS could be the warning shots across the bow to a coming realignment in the Indo-Pacific region. What will China do moving forward??
