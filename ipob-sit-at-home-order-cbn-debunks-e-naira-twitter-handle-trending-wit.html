In another development, social media was abuzz on Wednesday, following reports that the E-Naira speed wallet disappeared from Google Play Store after 100 thousand downloads. CBN claimed that the ‘speed wallet’ disappeared because an upgrade was needed.. and disclosed that they won’t bear the liability for losses caused by the #eNaira website.
