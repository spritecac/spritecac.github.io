Flash floods most often occur in dry areas that have recently received precipitation, but they may be seen anywhere downstream from the source of the precipitation, even many miles from the source. Flash floods are known to occur in the highest mountain ranges of the United States and are also common in the arid plains of the Southwestern United States.
