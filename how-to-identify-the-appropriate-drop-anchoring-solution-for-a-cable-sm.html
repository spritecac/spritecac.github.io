In this video Xavier, our technical expert, explains how to identify the appropriate drop anchoring solution for a selected cable. Discover our anchoring solutions on our website: https://www.telenco-networks.com/en

The choice of a drop anchoring solution is essential: it contributes to the deployment of a reliable and durable telecom network. This is why the drop anchoring solution must match the type of cable selected upstream.

In order to identify the most appropriate drop anchoring solution, 4 characteristics of the cable must be taken into account: its external diameter, the presence of FRP, its bending radius and the sheath’s density. It is also important to take into account that an indoor/outdoor cable does not have the same mechanical and environmental constraints as an indoor or outdoor cable.
In this video, Xavier shows you four drop anchoring solutions for three different fiber optic cables: indoor/outdoor cable Ø 6mm (TELENCO® DROPTIC LM3), outdoor cable Ø 4mm (TELENCO® DROPTIC LM8BK) and outdoor cable Ø 3mm (TELENCO DROPTIC® LM030PU).
