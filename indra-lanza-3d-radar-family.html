Lanza is a family of state-of-the-art 3D radar systems to operate in both fixed and deployable configurations.

Lanza LTR-25: Long Range Tactical Radar. Tactical configuration, deployable in less than 2 hours. NATO DADR (Deployable Air Defence Radar) -Class II- compliance. Instrumented range up to 250 NM. Mission: Back up of Air Defence network and force projection.

Lanza PSR (Primary Surveillance Radar) 3D: ATC (Air Traffic Control) Radar. Multiple operational modes at 150/100 NM instrumented range. Mission: Dual use, Surveillance and approach radar.

Lanza N: Naval Radar. 180/120/60 NM instrumented range. Mission: Anti-Air warfare capacity for naval platforms.
