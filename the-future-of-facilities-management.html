As we progress further into the 21st century and individuals incorporate technology into their daily lives, we become faster, more effective, and more efficient. This is the same for buildings. Technology is rapidly changing the roles and responsibilities of facilities management professionals. Connected devices, the Internet of Things, mobile technology, machine learning, and artificial intelligence (AI)-powered software are create a score of building maintenance, facility management, upkeep control, and meeting room management software that is cutting out a lot of wasted time and effort.

ARC Facilities’ integrated facilities management software is reconfiguring the built-space landscape and helping facilities managers respond faster, mitigate risks, keep costs down, and improve service with instant access to information. The technology of the digital revolution is creating a mobile first approach in the way we work. In order to keep satisfying customers and attracting the best staff, it’s essential organizations keep up.
