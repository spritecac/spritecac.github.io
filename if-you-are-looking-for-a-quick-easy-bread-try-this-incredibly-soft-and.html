This bread recipe is indeed a must try. The combination of butter, garlic, onion spring and cheese are indeniably good. It is so easy to make, you just need to proof the dough once and the end result will be incredible. The bread is so soft and chewy! BREAD FLOUR - ( 300g ) 2 1/2 cup flour
IINSTANT DRY YEAST - ( 5g )1 1/2 tsp
WARM MILK - ( 150g ) 3/4 cup
SUGAR - ( 25g ) 2 tbsp
SALT - ( 5g ) 1 tsp
MELTED BUTTER - ( 30g ) 1/8 cup

SPRING ONION - 1/8 cup green onion 15 g
