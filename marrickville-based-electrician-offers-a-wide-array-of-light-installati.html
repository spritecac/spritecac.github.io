Illustrating the company’s confidence in the quality of their electrical services, Ambient Electrical also offers an honest and transparent pricing scheme that is often cheaper than what their competitors offer. The owner Peter Stafford elaborates, “Electrical maintenance is an ongoing process, so we have no need to deliver shoddy service in the hopes you’ll call us again to repair the same fault later on. The job is always carried out in accordance with high industry standards of quality, and we firmly believe that this is what encourages customers to call on us again in the future. The system we repaired is unlikely to break quickly again once we see to it—but the modern home and office has many more systems to contend with. We’re always on hand if you need us later on.”
The company offers a wide selection of lighting options to perfectly fit everyone’s needs or requirements. As stated on their website, “Recessed Lighting such as the ‘Halogen downlight’, and the increasingly popular recessed LED lights are a great way to achieve that modern, sophisticated and vibrant atmosphere in the home. Whilst the Halogen Downlight has remained popular, the LED has become a cost effective choice in the long term.” They offer pendant lighting, which is a great form of lighting for setting a mood, and is really effective in homes with high ceilings. Pedestal lights are another form of lighting that is worth considering, as it offers a greater flexibility than the other options, making it possible to create an ideal setting.
See this video for more information: https://youtu.be/mF8lleNEk-Q
Other lighting options include sconce lights, directional lights and ceiling fans with lights. Sconce lights are often used to light up the hallway, as they provide light where light is often minimised. Directional lights on the other hand, are especially effective when illuminating photos, artworks, or noteworthy wall features. They are even more effective when paired with a dimmer switch like the Clipsal C-Bus or the Dynalite.
Ceiling fans with lights are the most common type of lighting sold by Ambient Electrical, in part due to the hot climate of Sydney. There is a lot of choice when it comes to selecting a style of fan that best suits a customer’s needs. Most ceiling fans come with a remote control, making it easy to regulate the fan speed and save on costs. With the use of a remote control, it’s possible to have a Fan/Light combination with no extra wiring required. This is especially good when the Fan will replace the existing Light in the middle of the room.
With Peter Stafford’s 16 years of experience in the field, he is able to offer a wide variety of electrical services. In addition to having electrical faults repaired, Sydney customers may request the installation of LED lights, ceiling fans, phone lines, smoke alarms, surge protectors, cooktops, and so on. Ambient Electrical is also able to carry out more generalised electrical inspections to ensure that a home or business’ electrical systems are functioning as expected. Notably, the company services a number of suburbs including Randwick, Double Bay, Zetland, Waverley, Coogee, Botany, and many more.
Interested parties may also wish to explore the company’s range of Sydney lighting installation services on offer.
