more notes on tiny habits
- When people say they want change they think big things
- The easiest most approachable way to making positive changes is tiny habits
- Disconnect between what people want and do and we tend to blame ourselves : Its not your fault, its not as hard as you think, not about motivation or willpower
- THINK SMALL, simple, easy expectations low . simplicity changes behavior
- It’s the approach thats the problem . aligns with human psychology
- Creating tiny positive habits is the most lasting way to create change
- Take a behavior you want, make it tiny. Find where it fits naturally in your life and nurture its growth
We feel like there is not enough time so we say no. 30 mins or 1 hr too much time a day so do nothing.
- A tiny habit is a small action you can do in less than 30 seconds
- Wire it in and grow . way to grow big is to start small . might feel insignificant but it allows you to gain momentum
- More stressed and less time you have the better this works .
Start immediately and dont try to be perfect

- Anchor behavior. Something you already do.
- B connect your new tiny habit behavior to it. Make it tiny
