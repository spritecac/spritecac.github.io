Gekatex and its subsidiaries develop, manufacture and distribute technical wiping, cleanroom wiping and polishing solution for Automotive OEM, Aeronautic, Electronics, Pharmaceutical and life science industries.

With 35 years of experience in the business of technical wiping, clean room wiping and polishing our solutions are recognized by many OEM manufacturer in their paint shop, clean room and assembly lines. We have a global presence with 3 factories: France, India and China that are ISO 9001 certified and a delivery center in Mexico for North America.

We serve more than 100 factories in the world through direct sales or distributors in more than 40 countries.
