During your education, it’s certain that someone has told you to write an outline for your essay before you write the essay itself. Often, you actually have to turn your outline in to your teacher or professor before you’re allowed to start writing the essay. But how do you write an outline? They never tell you how to do that! But that’s why we’re here. An outline is just a plan for your essay. Often, an outline will contain the idea you want to communicate in each paragraph. Let’s look at this in more detail!
