Twist lock clips for wires are ideal for temporarily securing wires and cables.

EdgeClips with a "twist lock" closure are quick and easy to use with one hand. The EdgeClip is particularly suitable when sticking, drilling or screwing or is not possible. Simply push it onto a plastic edge (1-3 mm edge thickness), then place the cable between the horns and temporarily fasten the cable by twisting the horns with your fingers.
One twist has up to 20 N of tensile strength (approx. 2 kg weight), plenty to hold a cable (6.5-8.5 mm) securely during temporary installations. The rounded edges protect fingers from cuts and the cable insulation from damage.
The Twist Lock closure can be closed and reopened hundreds of times, making it ideal for securing the leads of household appliances during transportation, or for fastening network cables or chains of lights at temporary events or installations.
