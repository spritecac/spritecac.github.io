Learn how to pronounce the the Suffix LY in Currently, Promptly, Previously, Usually correctly with this pronunciation lesson. The suffix LY is pronounced LEE. When a single letter Y ends a word, we typically say the long I sound. We hear this in sky, my, fly, try.
