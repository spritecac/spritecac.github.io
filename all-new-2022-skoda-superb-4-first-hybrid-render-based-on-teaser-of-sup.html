We tried to follow the provided teasers and illustrate the new Skoda Superb 2023 liftback with headlights and grill partly partly taken from last Skoda Octavia IV. - Powerdome and Skoda-typical grill;
- Crystalline design language;
- Crystalline C-shaped taillights;
