This week’s video features climbing legend Ned Feehally giving us an insight into his knowledge on all things climbing and training.
He covers his thoughts on how training can be used to help improve your climbing, briefly touches on his new book ‘Beastmaking’, and shows us how it’s done on some hard outdoor boulders.
