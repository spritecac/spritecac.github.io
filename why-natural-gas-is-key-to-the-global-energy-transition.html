Natural gas has overtaken coal and uranium as sources of electricity generation in OECD countries. In 2020, it accounted for a record 40% of electricity generation in the U.S. alone. With global electricity demand set for a sixfold surge due to the uptake of electric vehicles by 2030, natural gas is set to lead the global energy transition.
