Ever feel like your arrangements just aren’t quite right?⁠

You open up your project, but the task to re-arrange everything seems arduous, complicated and messy.
Well here’s a great solution for you…
⁠
Here’s my quick and easy way to experiment with your track’s arrangement in a way that is fun, intuitive, and doesn’t eat into your studio time.⁠
⁠
