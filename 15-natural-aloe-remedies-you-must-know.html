BEST HOMEMADE ALOE VERA RECIPES

Aloe vera gel is one of the cheapest beauty ingredients but it has a lot of benefits for skin and hair. Aloe vera leaves contain a lot of antioxidants and have an antibacterial effect. Aloe vera gel is an effective treatment for burns and sunburns. But the most important fact is that aloe vera gel can slow aging of the skin and improves skin elasticity. Besides, it hydrates and soothes skin. Antibacterial and antiseptic properties of aloe vera help to treat pimples and acne. Do not forget that aloe vera is also a brilliant ingredient for different hair masks and helps to cure dandruff, prevents split ends and stimulates hair growth.
Cellulite is a real disaster for most girls especially in summer when you want to wear shorts and short skirts. We are ready to share a treatment that helps to reduce the visibility of cellulite. In a bowl mix coconut oil, aloe vera gel and beat using a mixer. Apply this mixture daily after shower and rub the cream on cellulite affected areas.
One more surprising use of aloe vera juice is to cure stomach pain. It helps to control the secretion of acid in your stomach.
If you prefer deodorants with natural ingredients, you are going to love the next recipe. In a bowl mix olive oil, vegetable glycerin, aloe vera gel, and baking soda. Stir the mixture and warm up. Refrigerate and use!
Check out a perfect skin care recipe that will help to moisturize dry skin. Mix rose petals with aloe vera gel and yogurt. Apply this mask daily to effectively moisturize your skin.
Make a moisturizing soap for your hands from natural ingredients. Cut aloe leaves and mix them with basil using a blender. Add the mixture to the melted soap base and let cool.
Here is the best smoothie recipe for weight loss: mix cucumber with ginger, parsley, lemon juice, and aloe vera juice. Drink this smoothie before bedtime to lose belly fat.
