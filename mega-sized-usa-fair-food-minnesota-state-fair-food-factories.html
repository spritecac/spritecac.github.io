SWEET MARTHA’S COOKIES: Mix together secret dry mix and shortening. Pour in water, then add chocolate chips and mix. Send mixture to the dolloping machine. Dollop the dough. Place on tray. Place in the oven for 10-12 minutes. Remove and allow to cool a bit. Serve in a bucket.

FRESH FRENCH FRIES: Load the hopper with raw potatoes, send them up the conveyor belt to go through the peeler. Fry the french fries and season with a bit of salt.

3. CORN ROAST

ROASTED SWEET CORN: Soak corn for 10-15 minutes. Place on the grill for 10 minutes then flip it and let it roast another 5 minutes at 700 degrees F. Add water to steam it then husk. Dip it in a vat of salted melted butter. Enjoy.

💸PRICE: $4.00 USD per corn
