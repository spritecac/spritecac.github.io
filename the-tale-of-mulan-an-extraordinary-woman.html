In this video I will be taking you to 5th Century China where the Nomadic hoards of the Rouran were ever watch full, looking to take the lands of the Northen Wei dynasty. A young ordinary woman named Mulan would take her feeble fathers old war armor as her own and would take his place in the army of the son of heaven. This is her story.
