In this video I will be taking you to 5th Century China where the Nomadic hoards of the Rouran were ever watch full, looking to take the lands of the Northen Wei dynasty. The son of heaven or the emperor would call on the weak and the strong to war to battle the Nomadic hoards. This was a time for bravery, honor and warriors. A young ordinary woman named Mulan would take her feeble fathers old war armor as her own and would take his place in the army of the son of heaven. This is her story.
