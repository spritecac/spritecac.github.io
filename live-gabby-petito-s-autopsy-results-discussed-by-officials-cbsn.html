A Wyoming coroner on Tuesday is providing more details on the autopsy of Gabby Petito, a 22-year-old who was found dead after she went missing during a road trip with her fiancé Brian Laundrie. Petito’s body was discovered in Wyoming’s Grand Teton National Park in September and the initial manner of death was ruled a homicide.
