Inspired by Heaven’s Gate, the video proposes the idea of ‘what if they actually ascended?’ and is a faithfully accurate rebuilding of the infamous infinity projection shot that frames Marshall Applewhite in one of his final address speeches regarding their imminent exit to the "Next Level".
