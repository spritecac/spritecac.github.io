Charted at #2 in September 1951. Originally a #8 hit for Ella Fitzgerald with the Chick Webb Orchestra in 1939. Original Billboard review: "The classic jazz opus is done up Brown with the Ames’ pounding out the catchy and infrequently heard lyric. A splendid beat disking which could score, particularly in jukes and with jockeys". Overall Billboard rating: 87 out of 100.
