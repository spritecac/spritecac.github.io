In this episode, I look at What is Accreditation and What is Certification & how to use these terms correctly, when referring to ISO 14001:2015.

The video covers the definition of the terms: Accreditation and Certification from ISO/IEC 17000:2020 as well as the relationship between Accreditation Bodies, Certification Bodies and their Certified Clients including the use of ISO/IEC 17021-1:2015 as the International Standard for the accreditation of Certification Bodies issuing certification of Management System Standards (MSS), such as ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 9001:2015.

Simple guidance for the use of the terms: Accreditation and Certification is given to ensure that you can use the terms correctly.

I hope that this content will promote your own use of the terms: Accreditation and Certification.
