POSITIVE COMMUNITY VIBES, AND "NORMAL LIFE" IS BACK HERE IN PURARAN, CATANDUANES.

THANK YOU. There is a lot of appreciation around this community for the support everyone gave to the bayanihan home building and this community after three typhoons last year. This is just a simple video looking at a few homes and just checking up on the vibe in the community. The vibe is great. People have been busy working, living, and just being together as a community.

Being able to quickly put up homes, made a massive difference in not just this community, but many around the island.
