POSITIVE COMMUNITY VIBES, AND "NORMAL LIFE" IS BACK HERE IN PURARAN, CATANDUANES.

This is just a simple video looking at a few homes and just checking up on the vibe in the community. The vibe is great. People have been busy working, living, and just being together as a community.

Being able to quickly put up homes, made a massive difference in not just this community, but many around the island.
