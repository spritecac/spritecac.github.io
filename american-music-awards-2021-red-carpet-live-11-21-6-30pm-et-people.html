PEOPLE and Entertainment Weekly will count down to the American Music Awards with a live red carpet pre-show hosted by PEOPLE (the TV show!) correspondent Jeremy Parsons and People Every Day host Janine Rubenstein. The livestream will feature interviews with nominees as they arrive, as well as commentary around this year’s awards.

The AMAs will air live from the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles on Sunday at 8 p.m. ET/ 5 p.m. PT on ABC, and will stream the next day on Hulu. BTS and Coldplay will sing their chart-topping hit "My Universe" together live for the first time during the Cardi B-hosted show, and other performances include Olivia Rodrigo, Bad Bunny with Tainy and Juliete Venegas, Carrie Underwood and Jason Aldean, Kane Brown, New Edition, New Kids on the Block, Diplo, Måneskin, Mickey Guyton, Tyler, the Creator, Walker Hayes and Zoe Wees.
