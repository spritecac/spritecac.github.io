Here’s how to make a robe.
In this video, i show you how to make a satin robe suitable for brides and bridesmaids. This DIY robe tutorial is in two parts and In this part, I explain in details how I draft my robe patterns, the supplies needed and how to scale up or scale down to the size you want to make.

Please note that no seam allowance has been added to this pattern. All allowances will be added while cutting the fabric.
