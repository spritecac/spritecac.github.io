About Revolutionary Goans
Revolutionary Goans was a non-political organization formed to unite all Niz Goenkars irrespective of caste and religion to fight for the existence of Goans in their own motherland, to protect its identity, culture, and heritage. Our vision is to empower every person of Goan origin, fight for his/her rights, our own motherland.
What started off as an NGO in the year 2017 by Manoj Parab and the team has come a long way to form Political Party namely Revolutionary Goans Party with over 10,000 members and Huge Supports all around the Globe. We believe that passing the POGO Bill in assembly is the only solution to protect the rights of Niz-Goenkars.
