Welcome back to Euro Football Daily for this week’s edition of Euro Transfer Talk. The summer window may have closed but with free transfers available and the future of some high profile stars still uncertain, there’s plenty of stories to delve into in today’s episode.

We begin with Leeds lynchpin Kalvin Phillips, who is attracting interest from Manchester City. We then head to Old Trafford where Paul Pobga looks set to sign a new contract, inspired by the arrival of Cristiano Ronaldo. Then to our headline story, which is the news that Leicester City’s Youri Tielemans is on the radar of Barcelona and Liverpool, it’s thought that €60m could make a deal happen. Staying in Leicester for our next player, as Wesley Fofana has admitted it’s his dream to join Real Madrid. Our penultimate entry is RB Leipzig’s Dani Olmo, who is linked with Barcelona and Manchester United. And finally we finish our episode in Holland, where Ajax defender Jurrien Timber is the subject of a transfer battle between London rivals Chelsea and Spurs.
