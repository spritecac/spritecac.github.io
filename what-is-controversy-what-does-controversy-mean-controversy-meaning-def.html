Controversy is a state of prolonged public dispute or debate, usually concerning a matter of conflicting opinion or point of view. The most applicable or well known controversial subjects, topics or areas are politics, religion, philosophy, parenting and sex. History is similarly controversial. Other minor yet prominent areas of controversy are economics, science, finances, culture, education, the military, society, celebrities, organisation, the media, age, gender, and race.

Controversy in matters of theology has traditionally been particularly heated, giving rise to the phrase odium theologicum.
