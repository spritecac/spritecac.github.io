Hey everyone!

In this step by step tutorial I will be drawing a realistic rose with colored pencils from scratch 🌹 When you are done you will know how to draw a ROSE! This How to draw a rose-tutorial is for beginners and intermediates.

LEAVE A LIKE, COMMENT or SHARE to support the channel! Love from me to everyone who does that and I will answer as many as I can!

You can use this tutorial to create your own tattoo design, to make any sort of greeting cards or to draw it for your family and friends. Roses are beautiful flowers and I really love them!

To draw this you just need a few pencils and an eraser. I think, the hardest part is to sketch this complicated form of a rose, but I tried to explain and illustrate it. The colouring part is very simple and relatively fast! Use a reference picture or a real rose, when you draw your own!

I also decided to include a few fascinating facts about roses, I think you’ll like it! Did you know that a orange rose symbolizes enthusiasm?
