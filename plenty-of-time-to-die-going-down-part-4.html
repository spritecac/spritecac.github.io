The two most difficult levels of Going Down, so far. I like how the levels feel like small arenas where you have to fight these increasingly hard battles on. Space, ammo, and health are all scarce and if you don’t manage them well enough, you’ll end up dead, just like I did here… plenty!

Blind run: first time playing. Ultra-Violence, pistol start.
