Recount writing is an important skill for your students to learn. A Recount is when you write about something that actually happened, like a diary entry.

Your students will take their English Language writing skills to the next level with this video. Learn the features of a Recount, and take an exciting adventure to put those new found skills to the test.

I have made these educational videos for your kids to watch and learn from, as well as having some fun. I already have plenty of Lesson Videos that are already on my Youtube Channel, so there will be lots to learn!
