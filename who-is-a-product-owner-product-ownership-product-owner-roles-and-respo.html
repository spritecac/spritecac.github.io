Who is a Product Owner? In this video, we will understand the Product Ownership and the Product Owner roles and responsibilities. Whether you are a Scrum certified CSPO® or not, this video will tell you how to be a great Product Owner. By knowing the roles and responsibilities of the product owner, you will also be able to contribute better in the project management and product development process. No matter whether you are following Agile, Scrum, XP, Kanban, Lean or any other project management framework, being a good product owner is required for the success of the project.

Main roles and responsibilities of the product owner include-

Defining the vision
Managing the product backlog
Prioritizing needs
Overseeing development stages
Evaluating product progress at each iteration
