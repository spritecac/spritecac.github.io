This video teaches your child how to blend 3 letters together to read Short Vowel U 3 letter words.
This video shows your child to use both letter sounds and word families to help him/her blend 3 letters together to read words.
Reading 3 Letters words is taught by moving a Consonant closer and closer to Short Vowel U Word Family Endings.
Blending sounds to make words is a learning process. The first step of blending is when your child states the correct sounds for the letter characters that are being blended for example the word, "Bug" will often first be read as "B" - "ug." With practice your child will learn to further blend the sounds to correctly say "Bug."

Your child can also practice blending the sounds to read words with magnetic letters. Use the Short U Word families endings and add different Consonants to the front of the different word endings.
