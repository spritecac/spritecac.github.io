More About "CYBOLICA":
Three inmates awaken confused and shackled inside the dystopian super-prison Cybolica. When a lethal creature breaches containment, they must set aside their differences to break free and defend themselves. But after coming face to face with death, they realize the creature wasn’t the real “monster” as they were all destined to become what they’ve so desperately tried to escape.
