22 PURCHASING INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWERS:
Q1. Tell me about yourself?
Q2. Why do you want to work for our company in this purchasing role?
Q3. Talk me through the purchasing process.
Q4. What do you need to consider when buying a product?
Q5. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using overseas product suppliers?
Q6. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
Q7. What strategies do you use for discovering the best products?
Q8. How do you prioritize your workload?
Q9. Tell me about a time when you had to communicate a complex piece of information to someone who did not understand.
Q10. What do you look for in a supplier?
Q11. Tell me a time where you had to get your point across when you knew you were correct, and the other person was wrong.
Q12. What are the risks to a business in purchasing?
Q13. What skills and qualities do you possess that are a match for this purchasing role?
Q14. What’s the most challenging part of working in purchasing?
Q15. What’s been the toughest purchasing project you’ve ever been involved in?
Q16. Tell me about a purchasing project you’ve been involved in that did not go to plan. What happened and what did you learn from the experience?
Q17. What’s your style of communication?
Q18. Tell me a time when you used outstanding negotiation skills.
Q19. Why do you want to work in purchasing?
Q20. Tell me about a time when you resolved a difficult situation or problem at work.
Q21. Where do you see yourself in five years?
Q22. That’s the end of your purchasing interview. Do you have any questions for the panel?
