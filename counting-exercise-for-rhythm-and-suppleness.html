Rhythm and Suppleness is the base of the Dressage Training Scale (I sure hope you know that by now!!). Today’s video is a counting exercise that will help with rhythm AND suppleness. Believe it or not, counting out loud is a great way to find a steady tempo AND to help you and your horse relax. There is a big correlation between rhythm and relaxation!
