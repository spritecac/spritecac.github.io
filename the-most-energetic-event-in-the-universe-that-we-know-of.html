The amount of energy produced from the collapsing core of a massive star is pretty inconceivable. The mechanism that facilitates this the creation and distribution of this energy is just as wild. If you’re like me you may have heard that Supernovae are the result of stellar masses "bouncing" off the incredibly dense proto-neutron star core. Like many things in this world, reality is far more complicated.
