Sometimes, destiny can take us in a direction that we could never have expected. She and her family came to understand that there is something beyond mortal existence. In 2007 she began writing the manuscript which has now evolved into the House of Darkness House of Light trilogy. The time has come to disclose these secrets and the world is ready to receive them.
