you got recognized for success.
The diploma is recognition

You see, you were a success in your freshman year

You see, you’re a success all through school,
you’re a success every day,
success is a daily thing, not a destination thing.
The day you got the diploma you just got recognized
for the success that you already were.
Now, that’s very essential.
