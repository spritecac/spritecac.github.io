"So Far Away" is a rock single released in 1985 by British band Dire Straits. Appears on the album Brothers in Arms, and became the fourth hit of the band in the top 20 of Billboard, on reaching the 19th position. Then in 1996, the label released a compilation with the best songs of the band, "Sultans of Swing" and "So Far Away" was on track 8.
