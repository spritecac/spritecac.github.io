Our family got stuck in Anchorage after we got COVID. Everyone is fine but because we got it one at a time we spent several weeks unable to fly home. So after I got out of quarantine I went to Homer with my brother to rent a boat and go fishing for two days. we caught our limit of halibut and had a ball catching cleaning and cooking halibut. We caught the halibut on light tackle designed for salmon and rockfish which made it a load of fun.
