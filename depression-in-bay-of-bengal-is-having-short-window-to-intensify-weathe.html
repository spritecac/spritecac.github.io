Yesterday, Low pressure area has intensified into a depression and has moved towards north west wards and lies 320 km away from Odisha coast. The system is expected to track north west wards as it makes landfall near Balasore region on monday , September 13 , 2021 as a depression to deep depression state. The speed of the system is still expected to be around 45 to 55 kmph. However heavy rainfall will occur in various parts of central India in next 3-4 days.
