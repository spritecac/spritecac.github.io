Faith Chapel is a branch of Christ Healing Evangelical Church. A Church with international mission geared toward fulfilling the great commission of evangelizing the world for Christ. Our international Headquarters is located in Lagos Nigeria with branches all over the world.

It is our vision to use all resources at our disposal to raise up faithful men and women in Christ Jesus, who will in turn commit to raising others - 2 Timothy 2:2.

We strive to make the name of Jesus and the love of God real to all, promote the virtue of wisdom and understanding rooted in God’s word and to demonstrate that Jesus is still touching and changing lives.
