About Mr Paul Flora:

Mr Paul Flora is a Consultant Vascular Surgeon and he was the first surgeon in the UK to perform the Barostim procedure for the regulation of hypertension.

His subspecialist interests are in diabetic foot care, venous disease and venous thrombosis. He also has specific clinical interests in the treatment of deep venous disease, varicose veins (EVLT, Venefit, and glue), carotid disease, stroke, peripheral arterial disease, diabetic foot disease, vascular tumours and Thoracic outlet syndrome.
