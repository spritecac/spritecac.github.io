On September 14th, Funcom, the videogame developer and publisher behind both "Age of Conan", "Conan Exiles" and the upcoming "Dune", announced they acquired the Cabinet Group, and all its assests - including Conan the Cimmerian and the rest of the Robert E. Howard library.

Along with the acquisition though, comes Fredrik Malmberg, the head of Cabinet Group who will continue to manage all things Conan and Robert E. Howard as the head of the Funcom subsidiary Heroic Signatures. In this interview, he explains what this change actually entails, as well as providing an update on in-development projects.
