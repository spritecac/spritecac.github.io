NEW WAYS TO USE ORDINARY THINGS
Do you know that a shower cap can be extremely useful in your daily life? You can use shower caps to cover bicycle seats while raining, use it as shoe covers or protection for your gadget - we showed a lot of uses for shower caps you’ll really surprise. Moreover, here you’ll find life-changing everyday life hacks, unexpected uses for IKEA products and absolutely mind-blowing water tricks. Your life will never be the same after trying these unusual hacks!
Do you know how to surprise your friends? Make this easy experiment! You’ll need paprika, spice, oregano - add all ingredients to water and don’t mix. Take dishwashing liquid on the tip of cotton swab and immerse it to the water… You have to watch this video to know what will happen 😉😀
Besides, here you’ll find easy girly tricks that are really useful. We showed how to make eco-rubber from your hair and restore your stretched rubber using hot tea.
Another excellent hack - how to fix tips of shoelaces. It’s really easy: take a lighter and small piece of plastic straw. Put plastic straw on shoelace tips and cauterized it.
