Do you know that a shower cap can be extremely useful in your daily life? You can use shower caps to cover bicycle seats while raining, use it as shoe covers or protection for your gadget - we showed a lot of uses for shower caps you’ll really surprise. Moreover, here you’ll find life-changing everyday life hacks, unexpected uses for IKEA products and absolutely mind-blowing water tricks. Besides, here you’ll find easy girly tricks that are really useful. We showed how to make eco-rubber from your hair and restore your stretched rubber using hot tea.
