In an interview France’s former head of counter intelligence Yves Bonnet directly implicates President Mitterand in the Rainbow Warrior bombing. The incident caused a diplomatic brawl between New Zealand and France. Charles Hernu, the French Defence Minister at the time, was forced to resign but a French Government cover-up kept the President in the clear.
