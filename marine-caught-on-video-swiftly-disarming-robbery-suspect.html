James Kilcer, a U.S. Marine, was at a gas station in Arizona when three people entered the store and allegedly attempted to rob it. When a gun was pointed at Kilcer, he didn’t flinch. He disarmed the suspect and held him until police got there. "The Marine Corps taught me not to [mess] around," he said.
