Step 1: Solve the Cross
Step 2: Corners of the first layer.
Algorithm: R U R’ U’
Step 3: Second Layer.
Algorithms: (Left) L’ U’ L U y’ R U R’ U’

Algorithm: F (R U R’ U’) F’
Step 5: Solving Yellow.
Algorithm: R U R’ U R U2 R’
Step 6: Solving to the Dots.
Algorithm: R’ F R’ B2 R F’ R’ B2 R2
Step 7: Solving the Dots.
Algorithm: F2 U/U’ R’ L F2 R L’ U/U’ F2
