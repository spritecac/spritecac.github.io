By far, the best way to learn how to fast dance is to fast dance - a lot. However, having personal athleticism and agility to bring to the partnership is also a must. If you can’t physically move your own body fast, a partner will not make things any easier. In the absence of social dancing, events, and even partnership for many of us, this drill will help give you what you need to move quickly, so when fast dancing happens, you can focus on the partnership instead of on the breathlessness!
