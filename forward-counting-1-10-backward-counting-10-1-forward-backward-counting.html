What is to count?
In math, to count can be defined as the act of determining the quantity or the total number of objects in a set or a group.
In other words, to count means to say numbers in order while assigning a value to an item in the group, basis one to one correspondence.

Counting numbers are used to count objects.
Forward counting is counting by adding one more, every time.
Backward Counting – We can count down or back by saying numbers in reverse order while touching on each object once.
