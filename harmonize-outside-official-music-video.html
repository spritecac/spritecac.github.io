High School
After his fans named him the teacher of East African music “Teacher Konde”, Harmonize is presented as a Teacher on his second album, High School.

The album songs is his school subjects where he sings about life experiences including love, forgiveness, inspiration etc.

While he keeps his unique emotional East-African melodies, he continues to include more and more of the English language in his songs, as well as featuring artists like Naira Marley, Sarkodie, Busiswaah, Ibraah, Anjella & Sholo Mwamba.
