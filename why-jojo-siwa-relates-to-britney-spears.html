“Extra’s” Jenn Lahmers spoke with JoJo Siwa and Jenna Johnson after they performed to Britney Spears’ “…Baby One More Time” on “Dancing with the Stars.”

Siwa opened up about her connection to Britney, saying, “Being a child star, it’s hard for people to relate to or for other people to understand, so I can look to someone like her and see what she’s done and see the path that she’s taken, see what I like and what I don’t [like]… You learn from the people that come before you.”
