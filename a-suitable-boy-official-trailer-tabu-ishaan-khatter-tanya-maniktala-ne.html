Has love always been this difficult?

Vikram Seth’s written-world set in 1951 comes to life through Mira Nair’s imagination as Lata (Tanya Maniktala) grapples with just one wish: to choose her own husband.
Torn between family & her new romance, Lata embarks on a journey full of love & heartache, as three different men try to win her heart.

That’s when Maan Kapoor (Ishaan Khatter) walks in, accompanied by many romantic entanglements.
While India is getting the chance to come of age, will their love, too? Or is everything just too complicated?

Starring a magical ensemble cast featuring Tabu, Ishaan Khatter, Tanya Maniktala, Ram Kapoor, Vinay Pathak, Vijay Varma, Vijay Raaz, Shahana Goswami, Ranvir Shorey, Rasika Dugal, Randeep Hooda, Shubham Saraf, Mikhail Sen, Danesh Razvi, Mahira Kakkar, Namit Das, Manoj Pahwa, Aamir Bashir, Vivaan Shah: A Suitable Boy is a breathtaking story that’ll tug at your heart-strings and make you feel a love like never before.
