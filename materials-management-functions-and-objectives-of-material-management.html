This video is about Materials management.
Most manufacturing concerns spend more than 60% of the money they take in, on materials. That means materials soak up a substantial portion of the capital invested in the industry.
Material management involves controlling the type, amount, location, movement, timing of the purchase of various materials, etc.

1. What is materials management?
