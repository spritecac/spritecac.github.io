Today Cilla and Maddy are going to build things with pop its and then they can buy whatever they can build. How this works is that they will be timed a minute to build anything they can think of. In order to know how mandy DIY pop its they need, they must pick from a bowl a piece of paper, which gives them the amount of DIY pop its they are allowed to use. It was very difficult to figure out what to build in a short period of time, but Cilla and Maddy did it. Cilla and Maddy switched the pop its they built and then they had to buy for each other. It was very hard to guess what they built. Cilla and Maddy went by the shapes they built the pop its. There were so many shapes that were similar. However, this challenge was so much fun. It was pretty much a guessing game; you couldn’t really be right or wrong. Do you think they guessed the pop its right?
