The Charme V2 is 10x36 and was created for a couple from California. You get an amazing feeling as soon as you enter the front door. This unit feels airy, warm and you can definitely picture yourself living full time in this park model size tiny home. It features a main floor bedroom along with two other lofts for a capacity of 8 people sleeping if you include the sofa bed in the living area. The combination of materials really gives a strong character to this home.
