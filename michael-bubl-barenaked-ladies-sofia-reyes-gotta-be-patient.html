Michael heard the song, written by Spanish artists @STAY HOMAS, while self-quarantined with his family in Vancouver. He loved it and immediately shared it to his Facebook page. Michael will be donating his proceeds to the Red Cross in Canada and Argentina. Barenaked Ladies are donating their proceeds to the Canada Food Bank. Sofia will be donating her proceeds to various Latin charities.
