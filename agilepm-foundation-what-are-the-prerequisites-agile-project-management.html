Agile Project Management (AgilePM) is the world’s most popular agile project management framework. With its blend of Agile-style agility, flexibility, and adaptability with the structured control of the Dynamic Systems Development Method (DSDM), AgilePM offers a widely advantageous approach to successful project management. This has created a high demand for AgilePM-certified practitioners, both in terms of training staff and looking for managers with AgilePM qualifications and experience.

In this video, Claudine Peet explains what the prerequisites are for starting an accredited AgilePM Foundation course. While some background knowledge of Agile and project management can be helpful, there are no specific requirements for sitting the course.
