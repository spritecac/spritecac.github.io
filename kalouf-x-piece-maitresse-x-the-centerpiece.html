A new work in progress video watching French artist KALOUF ART executed at the "Zoo Art Show" in partnership with "La Pièce Maitresse".

Under the impetus of "the centerpiece" This delicately dangerous looking piano has exchanged its lyre for an audio system whose modernism completes the old aspect in a timeless and melodious object."
