Trade and Industry Minister Gan Kim Yong has said that allowing groups of five to dine together at the upcoming Bloomberg New Economy Forum will help boost networking at the event. Addressing the public backlash that followed news of the move, Mr Gan said the success of the event is important not just for the recovery of the MICE industry, but for Singapore’s "strategic position as a business hub".
