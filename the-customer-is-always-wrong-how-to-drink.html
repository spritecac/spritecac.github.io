Egg White Old Fashioned:
In Shaker
.25 oz. -or- 8 ml. Simple Syrup
2 dashes Angostura Bitters
2 oz. or 60 mo. Bulleit Rye
Egg white
Dry Shake
Add ice and shake
Double strain into glass
