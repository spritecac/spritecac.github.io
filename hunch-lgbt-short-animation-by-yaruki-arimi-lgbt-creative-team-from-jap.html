“Hunch（確信 Kaku-Shin）” is a bittersweet growing-up story of a gay student who has a crush on his classmate. This is also a story of the unrequited love that every LGBT teens would experience. It released in 2016, and it became popular among LGBT teens through social media. And it has been watched over 970,000 times on YouTube (as of November 2019).

/// What’s “YARUKI-ARIMI” ///
“YARUKI-ARIMI（やる気あり美）” is a creative team. The concept of the team is “bring LGBT into contact with worlds”. The team is made up of members who have various sexuality. They make various content such as articles, animation, playbooks.
