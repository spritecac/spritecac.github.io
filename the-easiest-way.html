This is my own little "Ho’oponopono-song". I borrowed the title of the song from Mabel Katz book of the same name.
Ho’oponopono is an ancient Hawaiian technique for solving problems. This song is about Self Identity through Ho’oponopono (SITH) which is an updated Ho’oponopono approach, a process of repentance, forgiveness and transmutation developed by Kahuna Lapa’au Morrnah Nalamaku Simeona.
Dr. Ihaleakala Hew Len often asks: "Have you ever noticed that whenever you have a problem, you are there?" I think it´s brilliant!!!
"Life is the dancer and you are the dance" is a quote from Eckhart Tolle´s book: A New Earth.
