WASSSUP! its me, Pluto. This song is VERY different, and allow me to explain the meaning. SO. I originally wrote this song as a joke, I thought of a scenario where a guy confesses his unrequited love for a girl, but after not getting any response, he jokingly says "or not, haha, just kidding, wow the weather is weird today. I was afraid 99% of the time. This blew my mind for some reason, I know it’s not a crazy concept but, lets just say how we feel.
