Join career and leadership coach and award-winning author Andrew LaCivita for today’s video on how to decide between multiple job offers! Whether you have multiple job offers in hand or are simply trying to choose between two companies to continue the interview process with, you need to have good decision-making mechanics!

In my experience as an executive recruiter and career coach, I’ve been involved with well more than a thousand people receiving new job offers. During most of these experiences, I worked directly with the employee as they thought through and negotiated their offers. Negotiation is one problem. The decision itself is something else entirely.
