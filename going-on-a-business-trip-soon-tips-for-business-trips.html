Today we’re talking about business trips.


What should you expect on a business trip?

Business trips are typically paid for by the company you work for, and if you play your cards right, you can even pocket some of the leftover money. Or, on the flip side, you can maximize all of the money you get to ensure you can turn this business trip into a vacation.

It all comes down to balancing work and play. While work may take up a lot of the trip, planning ahead and creating a schedule can help you fill your free time with action and adventure, or whatever else you find fun.
