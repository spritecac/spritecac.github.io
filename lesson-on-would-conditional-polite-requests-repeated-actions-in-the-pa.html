The modal verb "would" is the past of will, and is mostly used in the 2nd and 3rd conditionals. However, it is also used in other situations for example for polite requests. Mr. P. will list other situations where we could use "would". He will provide easy examples to understand when and how to use it.
This lesson is intended for intermediate students who wish to improve their English grammar, and for those above levels who wish to clear up doubts about this topic.
